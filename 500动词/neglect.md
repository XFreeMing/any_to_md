## 忽视

1. Moreover, say Wilson and Gilbert (2003), people neglect the speed and the power of their psychological immune system, which includes their strategies for rationalizing, discounting, forgiving, and limiting emotional trauma. (Myers, 2012)

> 此外，威尔逊和吉尔伯特（2003）说，人们忽视了他们的心理免疫系统的速度和力量，这包括他们合理化、折扣、宽恕和限制情感创伤的策略。

- neglect: 忽视
- moreover: 此外
- speed: 速度
- power: 力量
- psychological immune system: 心理免疫系统
- includes: 包括
- strategies: 策略
- rationalizing: 合理化
- discounting: 折扣
- forgiving: 宽恕
- limiting: 限制
- emotional trauma: 情感创伤

2. In the name of God, they denounced the greed and pomp of the heartless rich and the hypocrisy of pious Jews who worshiped in the prescribed manner but neglected their social obligations to their neighbor. (Perry, 2010)

> 以上帝的名义，他们谴责了无情的富人的贪婪和浮华，以及虔诚的犹太人的虚伪，这些犹太人按照规定的方式崇拜，但忽视了对邻居的社会义务。

- neglect: 忽视
- in the name of: 以...的名义
- denounced: 谴责
- greed: 贪婪
- pomp: 浮华
- heartless rich: 无情的富人
- hypocrisy: 虚伪
- pious Jews: 虔诚的犹太人
- worshiped: 崇拜
- prescribed manner: 规定的方式
- social obligations: 社会义务
- neighbor: 邻居

3. The humanists or intellectuals of the age considered their period (from the mid-fourteenth to the mid-sixteenth century) an age of rebirth, believing that they had restored arts and letters to new glory after they had been ''neglected'' or ''dead'' for centuries. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 那个时代的人文主义者或知识分子认为他们的时期（从14世纪中期到16世纪中期）是一个重生的时代，相信他们在被"忽视"或"死亡"了几个世纪之后，已经将艺术和文学恢复到新的辉煌。

- neglect: 忽视
- humanists: 人文主义者
- intellectuals: 知识分子
- age: 时代
- considered: 认为
- period: 时期
- mid-fourteenth: 14世纪中期
- mid-sixteenth century: 16世纪中期
- age of rebirth: 重生的时代
- believing: 相信
- restored: 恢复
- arts and letters: 艺术和文学
- new glory: 新的辉煌
- after: 在...之后
- dead: 死亡
- for centuries: 几个世纪

4. Long neglected during the papal exile in Avignon, Rome once more became a major artistic center in the late fifteenth century. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在教皇流亡阿维尼翁期间长期被忽视，罗马在15世纪后期再次成为主要的艺术中心。

- neglect: 忽视
- long: 长期
- during: 在...期间
- papal exile: 教皇流亡
- Avignon: 阿维尼翁
- Rome: 罗马
- once more: 再次
- became: 成为
- major artistic center: 主要的艺术中心
- late fifteenth century: 15世纪后期

4. Hours are long and they worry that they neglect their children. (Giddens, 2009)
