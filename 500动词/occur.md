1. In our examination of Western civilization, we need also to be aware of the dating of time. In recording the past, historians try to determine the exact time when events occurred.  (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在我们对西方文明的考察中，我们也需要了解时间的纪年。在记录过去时，历史学家试图确定事件发生的确切时间。

- occurred: 发生
- examination of Western civilization: 对西方文明的考察
- need to be aware of: 需要了解
- dating of time: 时间的纪年
- recording the past: 记录过去
- historians: 历史学家
- try to determine: 试图确定
- exact time: 确切时间
- events: 事件

2. The end of the last ice age around 10,000 B.C. was followed by what is called the Neolithic Revolution, a significant change in living patterns that occurred in the New Stone Age. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 大约公元前10,000年最后一个冰河时代的结束之后，出现了所谓的新石器革命，这是在新石器时代发生的居住模式的重大变化。

- occurred: 发生
- end of the last ice age: 最后一个冰河时代的结束
- around 10,000 B.C.: 大约公元前10,000年
- was followed by: 之后出现了
- what is called: 所谓的
- Neolithic Revolution: 新石器革命
- significant change: 重大变化
- living patterns: 居住模式
- New Stone Age: 新石器时代

3. Food shortages often occur in time of war. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 食物短缺经常在战争时期发生。

- occur: 发生
- food shortages: 食物短缺
- often: 经常
- in time of war: 在战争时期

4. The Israelites believed that during the Exodus from Egypt, when Moses supposedly led his people out of bondage into the "promised land," a special event occurred that determined the Jewish experience for all time. (Perry, 2010)

> 以色列人相信，在出埃及期间，当摩西据说带领他的人民从奴役中出来进入"应许之地"时，发生了一个特殊事件，这个事件决定了犹太人的永恒体验。

- occurred: 发生
- Israelites: 以色列人
- believed that: 相信
- during the Exodus: 在出埃及期间
- from Egypt: 从埃及
- Moses: 摩西
- supposedly led: 据说带领
- his people: 他的人民
- out of bondage: 从奴役中出来
- into the "promised land": 进入"应许之地"
- special event: 特殊事件
- determined: 决定
- Jewish experience: 犹太人的体验
- for all time: 永恒
