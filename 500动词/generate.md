1. For the next fifty years, Roman history would be characterized by two important features: the jostling for power by a number of strong individuals and the civil wars generated by their conflicts. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在接下来的五十年里，罗马历史将有两个重要特征：许多强有力个人之间的权力争夺以及由他们的冲突产生的内战。

- generate: 产生
- next fifty years: 接下来的五十年
- Roman history: 罗马历史
- characterized by: 以...为特征
- two important features: 两个重要特征
- jostling for power: 权力争夺
- number of: 许多
- strong individuals: 强有力个人
- civil wars: 内战
- conflicts: 冲突

2. England and France had developed strong national monarchies in the High Middle Ages. By the end of the fourteenth century, they seemed in danger of disintegrating due to dynastic problems and the pressures generated by the Hundred Years' War. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 英格兰和法国在中世纪盛期发展了强大的国家君主制。到14世纪末，由于王朝问题和百年战争产生的压力，它们似乎处于解体的危险中。

- generate: 产生
- England and France: 英格兰和法国
- developed: 发展
- strong national monarchies: 强大的国家君主制
- High Middle Ages: 中世纪盛期
- end of the fourteenth century: 14世纪末
- seemed in danger of: 似乎处于...的危险中
- disintegrating: 解体
- due to: 由于
- dynastic problems: 王朝问题
- pressures: 压力
- Hundred Years' War: 百年战争

3. As governments began to stabilize after the period of crisis, fewer magistrates were willing to accept the unsettling and divisive conditions generated by the trials of witches. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 随着政府在危机时期后开始稳定，更少的治安官愿意接受由女巫审判产生的不安和分裂的条件。

- generate: 产生
- governments: 政府
- began to stabilize: 开始稳定
- period of crisis: 危机时期
- fewer magistrates: 更少的治安官
- willing to accept: 愿意接受
- unsettling conditions: 不安的条件
- divisive conditions: 分裂的条件
- trials of witches: 女巫审判

4. Monarchs attempted to generate new sources of revenues, especially through taxes, which often meant going through parliaments. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 君主们试图产生新的收入来源，特别是通过税收，这通常意味着需要通过议会。

- generate: 产生
- monarchs: 君主们
- attempted to: 试图
- new sources of revenues: 新的收入来源
- especially: 特别是
- through taxes: 通过税收
- often meant: 通常意味着
- going through: 通过
- parliaments: 议会
