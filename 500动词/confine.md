1. More wretched still were the circumstances of the common people and, for a great part, of the middle class, for, confined to their homes either by hope of safety or by poverty, and restricted to their own sections, they fell sick daily by thousands. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 更悲惨的是普通民众的处境，在很大程度上也是中产阶级的处境，因为他们要么因为希望安全，要么因为贫困而被限制在家中，被限制在自己的区域，每天有数千人生病。

- confine: 限制，禁闭

- wretched: 悲惨的

- circumstances: 处境

- common people: 普通民众

- middle class: 中产阶级

- confined to: 被限制在

- homes: 家

- hope of safety: 希望安全

- poverty: 贫困

- restricted to: 被限制在

- sections: 区域

- fell sick: 生病

- daily: 每天

- thousands: 数千人

  

2. To limit contacts between Europeans and Chinese, the Qing government confined all European traders to a small island just outside the city walls of Canton and permitted them to reside there only from October through March. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 为了限制欧洲人和中国人之间的接触，清政府将所有欧洲商人限制在广州城墙外的一个小岛上，只允许他们在10月到3月期间居住在那里。

- confine: 限制

- limit contacts: 限制接触

- Europeans: 欧洲人

- Chinese: 中国人

- Qing government: 清政府

- European traders: 欧洲商人

- small island: 小岛

- city walls: 城墙

- Canton: 广州

- permitted: 允许

- reside: 居住

- October through March: 10月到3月

  

3. A police law empowered the police to arrest and confine anybody for political or nonpolitical crimes without due process of law. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 一部警察法授权警察可以在没有正当法律程序的情况下逮捕和监禁任何人，无论是因为政治犯罪还是非政治犯罪。

- confine: 监禁

- police law: 警察法

- empowered: 授权

- police: 警察

- arrest: 逮捕

- anybody: 任何人

- political crimes: 政治犯罪

- nonpolitical crimes: 非政治犯罪

- without: 没有

- due process of law: 正当法律程序

  

4. In Byzantine art, largescale statuary died out with the last imperial portraits, and stone carving was confined almost entirely to architectural ornament. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在拜占庭艺术中，大型雕像随着最后的帝国肖像而消失，石雕几乎完全局限于建筑装饰。

- confine: 局限于

- Byzantine art: 拜占庭艺术

- largescale statuary: 大型雕像

- died out: 消失

- imperial portraits: 帝国肖像

- stone carving: 石雕

- almost entirely: 几乎完全

- architectural ornament: 建筑装饰
