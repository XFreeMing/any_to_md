1. At the same time, the development of large standing armies ensured that political disputes would periodically be resolved by armed conflict rather than diplomacy. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 与此同时，大型常备军的发展确保了政治争端会定期通过武装冲突而不是外交来解决。

- resolve: 解决
- at the same time: 与此同时
- development: 发展
- large standing armies: 大型常备军
- ensured: 确保
- political disputes: 政治争端
- periodically: 定期地
- armed conflict: 武装冲突
- rather than: 而不是
- diplomacy: 外交

2. Protestant political leaders attempted to promote an alliance of the Swiss and German reformed churches by persuading the leaders of both groups to attend a colloquy (conference) at Marburg to resolve their differences.  (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 新教政治领导人试图通过说服两个群体的领导人参加在马尔堡举行的会议来解决他们的分歧，以促进瑞士和德国改革教会的联盟。

- resolve: 解决
- Protestant political leaders: 新教政治领导人
- attempted to: 试图
- promote: 促进
- alliance: 联盟
- Swiss and German reformed churches: 瑞士和德国改革教会
- persuading: 说服
- leaders: 领导人
- both groups: 两个群体
- attend: 参加
- colloquy: 会议
- conference: 会议
- Marburg: 马尔堡
- differences: 分歧

3. Aquinas first posed a question, cited sources that offered opposing opinions on the question, and then resolved them by arriving at his own conclusions. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 阿奎那首先提出一个问题，引用提供对该问题相反意见的来源，然后通过得出自己的结论来解决它们。

- resolve: 解决
- Aquinas: 阿奎那
- first: 首先
- posed: 提出
- question: 问题
- cited: 引用
- sources: 来源
- offered: 提供
- opposing opinions: 相反意见
- then: 然后
- arriving at: 得出
- own conclusions: 自己的结论
