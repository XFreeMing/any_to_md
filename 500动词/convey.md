1. I hope in this book to have conveyed some of the reasons why political philosophy has been an object of study and fascination for 2,500 years. But I also hope to have made clear that it is far from complete. (Wolff, 2006)

> 我希望在这本书中已经传达了一些为什么政治哲学在2500年来一直是研究和着迷的对象的原因。但我也希望已经清楚地表明，它还远未完成。

- convey: 传达
- hope: 希望
- book: 书
- reasons: 原因
- why: 为什么
- political philosophy: 政治哲学
- object of study: 研究的对象
- fascination: 着迷
- 2,500 years: 2500年
- but: 但
- also: 也
- made clear: 清楚地表明
- far from complete: 远未完成
