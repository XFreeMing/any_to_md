1. The Classical humanists of the Renaissance generated a deeper and more all-embracing appreciation of Greco-Roman antiquity than any of their predecessors. They regarded Classical authority not exclusively as a means of clarifying Christian truths, but as the basis for a new appraisal of the role of the individual in the world order. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 文艺复兴时期的古典人文主义者对希腊-罗马古代产生了比任何前辈都更深刻和更全面的欣赏。他们认为古典权威不仅仅是阐明基督教真理的手段，而且是重新评估个人在世界秩序中作用的基础。

3. Determining the function of the text often serves to clarify the nature of its form, and vice-versa. For instance, in that the Hebrew Bible, the Song of Roland, and many other early literary works were spoken or sung, rather than read, such literature tends to feature repetition and rhyme, devices that facilitate memorization and oral delivery. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 确定文本的功能往往有助于阐明其形式的性质，反之亦然。例如，希伯来圣经、《罗兰之歌》和许多其他早期文学作品是被说或唱的，而不是被阅读的，这样的文学往往具有重复和押韵的特点，这些手法有助于记忆和口头传达。

- function: 功能

- text: 文本

- serves to: 有助于

- nature: 性质

- form: 形式

- vice-versa: 反之亦然

- Song of Roland: 罗兰之歌

- literary works: 文学作品

- spoken or sung: 被说或唱的

4. As a second example of how experiments clarify causation, consider the correlation between television viewing and children's behavior. The more violent television children watch, the more aggressive they tend to be. (Myers, 2012)

> 作为实验如何阐明因果关系的第二个例子，考虑电视观看与儿童行为之间的相关性。儿童观看的暴力电视越多，他们往往越具有攻击性。
