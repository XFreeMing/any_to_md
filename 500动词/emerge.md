1. The theory of the "male gaze," which emerged among feminist critics of the 1960s, holds that the female image, usually conceived by male artists and rendered exclusively from the male perspective, reduces women to the status of objects. (Fiero, 2011)

> "男性凝视"理论，出现在20世纪60年代的女权主义批评家中，认为女性形象，通常由男性艺术家构思并完全从男性视角呈现，将女性降低到对象的地位。

- emerged: 出现
- theory of the "male gaze": "男性凝视"理论
- among: 在...中
- feminist critics: 女权主义批评家
- 1960s: 20世纪60年代
- holds that: 认为
- female image: 女性形象
- usually conceived by: 通常由...构思
- male artists: 男性艺术家
- rendered exclusively: 完全呈现
- from the male perspective: 从男性视角
- reduces women to: 将女性降低到
- status of objects: 对象的地位

2. During the Renaissance, dance emerged as an independent genre, and the first treatises were written on the art of dancing. (Fiero, 2011)

> 在文艺复兴时期，舞蹈作为独立体裁出现，第一批关于舞蹈艺术的论著被写出来。

- emerged: 出现
- during the Renaissance: 在文艺复兴时期
- dance: 舞蹈
- as an independent genre: 作为独立体裁
- first treatises: 第一批论著
- were written: 被写出来
- art of dancing: 舞蹈艺术

3. Fourteenth-century Europeans manifested an unprecedented preoccupation with differences in class, gender, and personality. Both in literature and in art, there emerged a new fidelity to nature and to personal experience in the everyday world. (Fiero, 2011)

> 十四世纪的欧洲人表现出对阶级、性别和个性差异的前所未有的关注。在文学和艺术中，都出现了对自然和日常生活中个人体验的新忠诚。

- emerged: 出现
- fourteenth-century Europeans: 十四世纪的欧洲人
- manifested: 表现出
- unprecedented preoccupation: 前所未有的关注
- differences in: ...的差异
- class: 阶级
- gender: 性别
- personality: 个性
- both in literature and in art: 在文学和艺术中
- there emerged: 出现了
- new fidelity: 新忠诚
- to nature: 对自然
- personal experience: 个人体验
- everyday world: 日常生活

4. The language of everyday speech was favored for such literary genres as the medieval romance and the more realistic and satiric picaresque novel, which emerged as a popular form of literary entertainment in sixteenth-century Spain. (Fiero, 2011)

> 日常口语被用于中世纪浪漫小说和更现实、更具讽刺性的流浪汉小说等文学体裁，后者在十六世纪的西班牙作为流行的文学娱乐形式出现。

- emerged: 出现
- language of everyday speech: 日常口语
- was favored for: 被用于
- literary genres: 文学体裁
- medieval romance: 中世纪浪漫小说
- more realistic: 更现实的
- satiric: 讽刺性的
- picaresque novel: 流浪汉小说
- as a popular form: 作为流行形式
- literary entertainment: 文学娱乐
- sixteenth-century Spain: 十六世纪的西班牙
