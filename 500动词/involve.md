1. As it happened, the whole town had turned out to attend the sensational trial that involved a lady of such beauty and fame, and when the people heard her roguish question, they burst into a roar of laughter, shouting to a man that she was right and had spoken well. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 碰巧，整个城镇的人都出来参加这场涉及一位如此美丽和著名的女士的轰动性审判，当人们听到她顽皮的问题时，他们爆发出阵阵笑声，异口同声地喊道她是对的，说得好。

- involve: 涉及
- as it happened: 碰巧
- whole town: 整个城镇
- turned out: 出来
- attend: 参加
- sensational: 轰动性的
- trial: 审判
- lady: 女士
- beauty: 美丽
- fame: 名声
- roguish: 顽皮的
- question: 问题
- burst into: 爆发出
- roar of laughter: 阵阵笑声
- shouting to a man: 异口同声地喊道
- spoken well: 说得好

2. Documents show increasing numbers of women involved in managing slaves, selling property, and making loans. (Spielvogel, 2010)

- involve: 参与
- documents: 文件
- show: 显示
- increasing numbers: 不断增加的数量
- women: 妇女
- managing: 管理
- slaves: 奴隶
- selling: 出售
- property: 财产
- making loans: 放贷

- played: 发挥
- energetic role: 积极的作用
- government: 政府

> 也许所有公民都应该参与决定最重要的"特定行政行为"，特别是当我们记住，卢梭认为宣战是行政部门的行动，而不是主权者的行动。

- involve: 参与
- perhaps: 也许
- all citizens: 所有公民
- should be: 应该
- deciding: 决定
- most important: 最重要的
- particular acts: 特定行为
- administration: 行政
- especially: 特别是
- remember: 记住
- declaring war: 宣战
- considered: 认为
- Rousseau: 卢梭
- action: 行动
- executive: 行政部门
- Sovereign: 主权者
