1. Regardless of their origins, kings had power---they led armies, initiated legislation, supervised the building of public works, provided courts, and organized workers for the irrigation projects on which Mesopotamian agriculture depended. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 无论他们的起源如何，国王都有权力——他们领导军队，发起立法，监督公共工程的建设，提供法庭，并为美索不达米亚农业所依赖的灌溉项目组织工人。

- initiated: 发起
- regardless of: 无论
- their origins: 他们的起源
- kings: 国王
- had power: 有权力
- led armies: 领导军队
- legislation: 立法
- supervised: 监督
- building of public works: 公共工程的建设
- provided courts: 提供法庭
- organized workers: 组织工人
- irrigation projects: 灌溉项目
- Mesopotamian agriculture: 美索不达米亚农业
- depended: 依赖

2. To eliminate such conditions, a number of countries have legally recognized the right to an adequate standard of space for movement, grazing, and exercise for farm animals. For example, in 1988 Sweden initiated a law that addresses unjust confinement and recognizes rights of the members of species such as cattle, pigs, and chickens. (Beauchamp , 2011)

> 为了消除这些条件，一些国家已经法律上承认了农场动物在运动、放牧和锻炼方面拥有足够空间标准的权利。例如，1988年瑞典发起了一项法律，该法律处理不公正的监禁，并承认牛、猪和鸡等物种成员的权利。

- initiated: 发起
- to eliminate: 为了消除
- such conditions: 这些条件
- a number of countries: 一些国家
- legally recognized: 法律上承认
- right to: ...的权利
- adequate standard: 足够标准
- space for movement: 运动空间
- grazing: 放牧
- exercise: 锻炼
- farm animals: 农场动物
- for example: 例如
- Sweden: 瑞典
- law: 法律
- addresses: 处理
- unjust confinement: 不公正的监禁
- recognizes rights: 承认权利
- members of species: 物种成员
- cattle: 牛
- pigs: 猪
- chickens: 鸡

- initiated: 发起
- in raising: 在组建
- new army: 新军队
- Marius: 马略
- military reforms: 军事改革
- proved to have: 被证明具有
- drastic consequences: 严重后果
