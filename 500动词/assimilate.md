1. During its height, the Hittite Empire also demonstrated an interesting ability to assimilate other cultures into its own. In languages, literature, art, law, and religion, the Hittites borrowed much from Mesopotamia as well as the native peoples that they had subdued. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在其鼎盛时期，赫梯帝国也展示了将其他文化同化到自己的有趣能力。在语言、文学、艺术、法律和宗教方面，赫梯人从美索不达米亚以及他们征服的土著民族那里借用了很多。

- Hittite Empire: 赫梯帝国

- interesting ability: 有趣能力
- other cultures: 其他文化
- into its own: 到自己的
- languages: 语言
- literature: 文学
- art: 艺术
- law: 法律
- religion: 宗教
- Hittites: 赫梯人
- borrowed much from: 从...借用了很多
- Mesopotamia: 美索不达米亚
- native peoples: 土著民族
- subdued: 征服

2. In a number of large-scale paintings for Venetian confraternities, groups of laypeople organized for religious activities, he assimilates the visionary effects of Titian's late paintings and the energetic compositions of the late Michelangelo. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在为威尼斯兄弟会（为宗教活动组织的平信徒团体）创作的一些大型绘画中，他吸收了提香晚期绘画的梦幻效果和晚期米开朗基罗的充满活力的构图。

- assimilate: 吸收
- number of: 一些
- large-scale paintings: 大型绘画

- Assyrian culture: 亚述文化
- hybrid: 混合的
