1. senate: the leading council of the Roman Republic; composed of about three hundred men (senators) who served for life and dominated much of the political life of the Republic. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 元老院：罗马共和国的领导委员会；由大约三百名终身任职并主导共和国大部分政治生活的男子（元老）组成。
