1. The major elements of music are melody, rhythm, harmony, and tone color—formal elements that also characterize the oral life of literature. (Fiero, 2011a)

> 音乐的主要元素是旋律、节奏、和声和音色——这些形式元素也表征了文学的口头生活。

- characterize: 表征，以...为特征

- major elements: 主要元素

- music: 音乐

- melody: 旋律

- rhythm: 节奏

- harmony: 和声

- tone color: 音色

- formal elements: 形式元素

- oral life: 口头生活

- literature: 文学

  

2. The surprising transience implicit in the children's youthfulness and animated gestures on the altar also characterizes a one-third-life-size portrait of Akhenaten's mother Queen Tiy, chief wife of Amenhotep III. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在祭坛上孩子们青春和生动姿态中隐含的令人惊讶的短暂性也表征了阿肯那顿母亲提伊王后（阿蒙霍特普三世的主要妻子）的三分之一真人大小肖像。

- characterize: 表征，以...为特征

- surprising: 令人惊讶的

- transience: 短暂性

- implicit: 隐含的

- youthfulness: 青春

- animated gestures: 生动姿态

- altar: 祭坛

- one-third-life-size: 三分之一真人大小

- portrait: 肖像

- chief wife: 主要妻子

  

3. In its most general sense, a society is a collection of people who occupy the same territorial area. However, not every group of people constitutes a society. Societies are characterized by regular patterns of social interaction. (Heywood, 2013)

> 在最一般的意义上，社会是占据同一领土区域的人的集合。然而，并非每一群人构成一个社会。社会以规律的社会互动模式为特征。

- characterize: 以...为特征

- most general sense: 最一般的意义

- society: 社会

- collection: 集合

- occupy: 占据

- territorial area: 领土区域

- group of people: 一群人

- constitutes: 构成

- regular patterns: 规律模式

- social interaction: 社会互动

  

4. Society in the Early Roman Empire was characterized by a system of social stratification, inherited from the Republic, in which Roman citizens were divided into three basic classes: the senatorial, equestrian, and lower classes.

> 早期罗马帝国的社会以社会分层制度为特征，这一制度继承自共和国，其中罗马公民被分为三个基本阶级：元老院阶级、骑士阶级和下层阶级。

- characterize: 以...为特征

- Early Roman Empire: 早期罗马帝国

- system: 制度

- social stratification: 社会分层

- inherited: 继承

- Republic: 共和国

- Roman citizens: 罗马公民

- divided into: 分为

- basic classes: 基本阶级

- senatorial: 元老院的

- equestrian: 骑士的

- lower classes: 下层阶级
