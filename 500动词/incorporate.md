1. At the beginning of the twenty-first century, the EU has established a new goal: to incorporate into the union the states of eastern and southeastern Europe. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在21世纪初，欧盟确立了一个新目标：将东欧和东南欧的国家纳入联盟。

- incorporate: 纳入
- at the beginning of: 在...开始时
- twenty-first century: 21世纪
- EU: 欧盟
- established: 确立
- new goal: 新目标
- union: 联盟
- states: 国家
- eastern Europe: 东欧
- southeastern Europe: 东南欧

2. Jazz is primarily a performer's rather than a composer's art. Dominated by Afro-Caribbean rhythmic styles, it incorporates a wide range of European and African-American concepts of harmony, melody, and tone color. (Fiero, 2011f)

> 爵士乐主要是表演者的艺术而不是作曲家的艺术。由非裔加勒比节奏风格主导，它融合了广泛的欧洲和非裔美国人的和声、旋律和音色概念。

- incorporate: 融合
- jazz: 爵士乐
- primarily: 主要地
- performer's art: 表演者的艺术
- rather than: 而不是
- composer's art: 作曲家的艺术
- dominated by: 由...主导
- Afro-Caribbean rhythmic styles: 非裔加勒比节奏风格
- wide range: 广泛的
- European concepts: 欧洲概念
- African-American concepts: 非裔美国人概念
- harmony: 和声
- melody: 旋律
- tone color: 音色

3. Modernists incorporated silence in their compositions, much as Cubist sculptors introduced negative space into mass. The results were as startling to the ear as Cubism was to the eye.  (Fiero, 2011f)

> 现代主义者在他们的作品中融入了沉默，就像立体主义雕塑家将负空间引入体量一样。结果对耳朵来说就像立体主义对眼睛一样令人震惊。

- incorporate: 融入
- modernists: 现代主义者
- silence: 沉默
- compositions: 作品
- much as: 就像
- Cubist sculptors: 立体主义雕塑家
- introduced: 引入
- negative space: 负空间
- mass: 体量
- results: 结果
- startling: 令人震惊
- ear: 耳朵
- Cubism: 立体主义
- eye: 眼睛

4. A communist planned economy could incorporate these people into production, improving efficiency and reducing the working day. These people include not only the unemployed, but members of the police and armed forces, the clergy, domestic servants, and, most despised of all, 'speculating, swindling superfluous middlemen, who have forced themselves in between the producer and the consumer'. (Wolff, 2006)

> 共产主义计划经济可以将这些人纳入生产，提高效率并减少工作日。这些人不仅包括失业者，还包括警察和武装部队成员、神职人员、家庭佣人，以及最令人鄙视的"投机、诈骗的多余中间商，他们强行介入生产者和消费者之间"。

- incorporate: 纳入
- communist planned economy: 共产主义计划经济
- these people: 这些人
- production: 生产
- improving efficiency: 提高效率
- reducing: 减少
- working day: 工作日
- include: 包括
- not only: 不仅
- unemployed: 失业者
- members: 成员
- police: 警察
- armed forces: 武装部队
- clergy: 神职人员
- domestic servants: 家庭佣人
- most despised of all: 最令人鄙视的
- speculating: 投机
- swindling: 诈骗
- superfluous middlemen: 多余的中间商
- forced themselves: 强行介入
- producer: 生产者
- consumer: 消费者
