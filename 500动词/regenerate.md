1. Most members of the Third Estate advocated a regular constitutional government that would abolish the fiscal privileges of the church and nobility as the major way to regenerate France. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 第三等级的大多数成员主张建立一个常规的宪政政府，废除教会和贵族的财政特权，作为复兴法国的主要方式。

- regenerate: 复兴
- most members: 大多数成员
- Third Estate: 第三等级
- advocated: 主张
- regular constitutional government: 常规的宪政政府
- abolish: 废除
- fiscal privileges: 财政特权
- church: 教会
- nobility: 贵族
- major way: 主要方式
- France: 法国

2. Such processes as recycling land and existing buildings, improving the urban environment, managing local areas better and with the participation of local citizens, and using public funds both to regenerate the area and to attract further private investment. (Giddens, 2009)

> 这样的过程，如回收土地和现有建筑，改善城市环境，更好地管理地方区域并让当地公民参与，以及使用公共资金既复兴该地区又吸引进一步的私人投资。

- regenerate: 复兴
- such processes as: 这样的过程，如
- recycling: 回收
- land: 土地
- existing buildings: 现有建筑
- improving: 改善
- urban environment: 城市环境
- managing: 管理
- local areas: 地方区域
- participation: 参与
- local citizens: 当地公民
- using public funds: 使用公共资金
- attract: 吸引
- further private investment: 进一步的私人投资

3.
