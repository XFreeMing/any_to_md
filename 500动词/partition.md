1. In eastern and central Europe especially the choice seemed increasingly to lie between partitioning or being partitioned.

> 特别是在东欧和中欧，选择似乎越来越在于分割或被分割。

- partition: 分割
- eastern Europe: 东欧
- central Europe: 中欧
- especially: 特别是
- choice: 选择
- seemed increasingly: 似乎越来越
- lie between: 在于
- partitioning: 分割
- being partitioned: 被分割

2. During the sixth and seventh centuries, the once-united Frankish kingdom came to be partitioned into three major areas: Neustria in northern Gaul; Austrasia, consisting of the ancient Frankish lands on both sides of the Rhine; and the former kingdom of Burgundy. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在6世纪和7世纪期间，曾经统一的法兰克王国被分割成三个主要地区：高卢北部的纽斯特里亚；奥斯特拉西亚，包括莱茵河两岸的古代法兰克土地；以及前勃艮第王国。

- partition: 分割
- during: 在...期间
- sixth and seventh centuries: 6世纪和7世纪
- once-united: 曾经统一的
- Frankish kingdom: 法兰克王国
- came to be: 被
- three major areas: 三个主要地区
- Neustria: 纽斯特里亚
- northern Gaul: 高卢北部
- Austrasia: 奥斯特拉西亚
- consisting of: 包括
- ancient Frankish lands: 古代法兰克土地
- both sides of the Rhine: 莱茵河两岸
- former kingdom of Burgundy: 前勃艮第王国

3. World standard time was first introduced in 1884 at a conference of nations held in Washington. The globe was then partitioned into 24 time zones, each one hour apart, and an exact beginning of the universal day was fixed. (Giddens, 2009)

> 世界标准时间于1884年在华盛顿举行的国家会议上首次引入。然后地球被分割成24个时区，每个时区相隔一小时，并确定了世界日的精确开始。

- partition: 分割
- world standard time: 世界标准时间
- first introduced: 首次引入
- 1884: 1884年
- conference of nations: 国家会议
- held in: 在...举行
- Washington: 华盛顿
- globe: 地球
- then: 然后
- 24 time zones: 24个时区
- each one hour apart: 每个相隔一小时
- exact beginning: 精确开始
- universal day: 世界日
- fixed: 确定

- resources: 资源
- not partitioned: 没有被分割
- people: 人们
- often: 经常
- consume: 消费
