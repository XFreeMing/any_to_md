1. The flowers she holds are a symbol of fertility, faithfulness, and feminine grace, indicating that she is a good mother and wife, and a charming woman. Her expensive dress was imported from London, as was her necklace. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 她拿着的花是生育、忠诚和女性优雅的象征，表明她是一个好母亲和好妻子，也是一个迷人的女人。她昂贵的连衣裙是从伦敦进口的，她的项链也是。

- import: 进口
- flowers: 花
- holds: 拿着
- symbol: 象征
- fertility: 生育
- faithfulness: 忠诚
- feminine grace: 女性优雅
- indicating: 表明
- good mother: 好母亲
- wife: 妻子
- charming woman: 迷人的女人
- expensive dress: 昂贵的连衣裙
- London: 伦敦
- necklace: 项链

2. Gregg Easterbrook (2004) noted that if the United States had raised its gasoline tax by 50 cents a decade ago, as was proposed, the country would now have smaller, more fuel-efficient cars (as do the Europeans, with their higher petrol taxes) and would therefore import less oil. (Myers, 2012)

> 格雷格·伊斯特布鲁克（2004）指出，如果美国在十年前将其汽油税提高50美分，正如所提议的那样，这个国家现在将拥有更小、更省油的汽车（就像欧洲人一样，他们的汽油税更高），因此将进口更少的石油。

- import: 进口
- Gregg Easterbrook: 格雷格·伊斯特布鲁克
- noted: 指出
- if: 如果
- United States: 美国
- raised: 提高
- gasoline tax: 汽油税
- 50 cents: 50美分
- decade ago: 十年前
- as was proposed: 正如所提议的
- country: 国家
- would now have: 现在将拥有
- smaller: 更小的
- more fuel-efficient cars: 更省油的汽车
- Europeans: 欧洲人
- higher petrol taxes: 更高的汽油税
- therefore: 因此
- less oil: 更少的石油

- much the same way: 几乎相同的方式

4. Most poor countries lack even a science adviser to their government. Moreover, these countries are too poor to import computers, mobile phones, fax machines, computerized factory machinery or other kinds of high technology. (Giddens, 2009)

> 大多数贫穷国家甚至缺乏政府科学顾问。此外，这些国家太穷，无法进口计算机、手机、传真机、计算机化工厂机械或其他类型的高科技。

- import: 进口
- most poor countries: 大多数贫穷国家
- lack: 缺乏
- even: 甚至
- science adviser: 科学顾问
- government: 政府
- moreover: 此外
- these countries: 这些国家
- too poor: 太穷
- computers: 计算机
- mobile phones: 手机
- fax machines: 传真机
- computerized factory machinery: 计算机化工厂机械
- other kinds: 其他类型
