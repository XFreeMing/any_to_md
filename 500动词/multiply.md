## 乘以；乘法

1. What do you get if you multiply six by nine? (Longman Dictionary of Contemporary English, 2015)

> 六乘以九等于多少？

- multiply: 乘以，乘法
- six: 六
- nine: 九

2. Calculating the expected utility of the mussels is slightly harder work. What we do is take the utility of each possible outcome and multiply it by its probability. (Wolff, 2006)

> 计算贻贝的预期效用是稍微困难的工作。我们所做的是取每个可能结果的效用，并将其乘以它的概率。

- multiply: 乘以
- calculating: 计算
- expected utility: 预期效用
- mussels: 贻贝
- slightly harder: 稍微困难
- take: 取
- utility: 效用
- each possible outcome: 每个可能的结果
- probability: 概率

3. The most basic method of establishing a proportional order in building was to either multiply or to divide a unit of measure, a system employed in the ninth century in the St. Gall monastery plan... (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在建筑中建立比例秩序的最基本方法是乘以或除以一个测量单位，这是九世纪在圣加仑修道院计划中使用的系统...

- multiply: 乘以
- most basic method: 最基本的方法
- establishing: 建立
- proportional order: 比例秩序
- building: 建筑
- either...or: 要么...要么
- divide: 除以
- unit of measure: 测量单位
- system: 系统
- employed: 使用
- ninth century: 九世纪
- St. Gall monastery: 圣加仑修道院
- plan: 计划

4. Children will learn to multiply in the second grade.

> 孩子们将在二年级学习乘法。

- multiply: 乘法
- children: 孩子们
- learn: 学习
- second grade: 二年级
