1. The Greeks created a tradition of warfare that became a prominent element of Western civilization. For example, the Greeks devised excellent weapons and body armor, making effective use of technological improvements. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 希腊人创造了战争传统，这成为西方文明的一个突出元素。例如，希腊人设计了优秀的武器和盔甲，有效利用了技术进步。

- devised: 设计
- Greeks: 希腊人
- created: 创造
- tradition of warfare: 战争传统
- prominent element: 突出元素
- Western civilization: 西方文明
- for example: 例如
- excellent weapons: 优秀的武器
- body armor: 盔甲
- making effective use of: 有效利用
- technological improvements: 技术进步

2. To encourage this trade, the counts (伯爵) of Champagne in northern France devised a series of six fairs held annually in the chief towns of their territory. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 为了鼓励这种贸易，法国北部香槟的伯爵设计了一系列六个集市，每年在他们的领土主要城镇举行。

- devised: 设计
- to encourage: 为了鼓励
- this trade: 这种贸易
- counts: 伯爵
- Champagne: 香槟
- northern France: 法国北部
- series of six fairs: 一系列六个集市
- held annually: 每年举行
- chief towns: 主要城镇
- their territory: 他们的领土

3. Southern France was devastated, but Catharism remained, which caused the Catholic church to devise a regular method for discovering and dealing with heretics. (Spielvogel, 2010)

- devise: 设计
- southern France: 法国南部
- devastated: 被摧毁
- Catharism: 清洁派
- remained: 仍然存在
- caused: 导致
- Catholic church: 天主教会
- regular method: 定期方法
- discovering: 发现
- dealing with: 处理
- heretics: 异端

- devised: 设计
- in math: 在数学中
- Sumerians: 苏美尔人
- number system: 数字系统
- based on 60: 基于60
- using combinations: 使用组合
- of 6 and 10: 6和10的
