1. Because of its unique pattern of development in the High Middle Ages, the German monarchy had become established on an elective rather than hereditary basis. This principle of election was standardized in 1356 by the Golden Bull issued by Emperor Charles IV (1346--1378). (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 由于在中世纪盛期独特的发展模式，德国君主制建立在选举而非世袭的基础上。这一选举原则在1356年由皇帝查理四世（1346-1378）颁布的《金玺诏书》标准化。
