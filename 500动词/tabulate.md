1. Historical migrations of publicly traded debt instruments, such as corporate bonds, are used to tabulate the annual probability of any given change in credit risk. (Berger, Molyneux, & Wilson, 2010)

> 公开交易的债务工具（如公司债券）的历史迁移被用来制表显示任何给定信用风险变化的年度概率。

- tabulate: 制表
- historical migrations: 历史迁移
- publicly traded debt instruments: 公开交易的债务工具
- corporate bonds: 公司债券
- used to: 被用来
- annual probability: 年度概率
- given change: 给定变化
- credit risk: 信用风险

2. A spreadsheet that tabulates all the distances between pairs of sizable cities in the U.S. contains the same information as the corresponding map of the U.S., but the spreadsheet gives you no idea what the U.S. looks like. (Poole, 2005)

> 一个制表显示美国所有大型城市对之间距离的电子表格包含与美国相应地图相同的信息，但电子表格让你无法了解美国是什么样子。

- tabulate: 制表
- spreadsheet: 电子表格
- distances: 距离
- pairs: 对
- sizable cities: 大型城市
- U.S.: 美国
- contains: 包含
- same information: 相同信息
- corresponding map: 相应地图
- gives you no idea: 让你无法了解
- looks like: 是什么样子

- tabulate: 制表
- Google Scholar: 谷歌学术
- no longer allows: 不再允许
- division by field: 按领域划分
- able to: 能够
- number of articles: 文章数量
- contained: 包含
- either: 或者
- word: 词
- economics: 经济学
- finance: 金融

4. Genetics brings something different to the comparison. A DNA sequence is a one-dimensional entity, a long series of A, G, C, and T subunits. Align two sequences from different species and you can simply tabulate their similarities; if they match 98 out of 100 times, then the species are 98 percent genetically identical. (Haviland, Prins, McBride, & Walrath, 2013)

> 遗传学为比较带来了不同的东西。DNA序列是一维实体，是A、G、C和T亚基的长序列。对齐来自不同物种的两个序列，你可以简单地制表显示它们的相似性；如果它们在100次中匹配98次，那么这些物种在遗传上98%相同。

- tabulate: 制表
- genetics: 遗传学
- brings something different: 带来不同的东西
- comparison: 比较
- DNA sequence: DNA序列
- one-dimensional entity: 一维实体
- long series: 长序列
- A, G, C, and T subunits: A、G、C和T亚基
- align: 对齐
- sequences: 序列
- different species: 不同物种
- simply: 简单地
- similarities: 相似性
- match: 匹配
- 98 out of 100 times: 100次中98次
- species: 物种
- 98 percent: 98%
- genetically identical: 遗传上相同
