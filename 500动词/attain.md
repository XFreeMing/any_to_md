1. The antihero was an important new kind of hero, one who established personal moral codes and followed personal passions to attain freedom and fulfill individual needs. These values would become part of the foundation of Fuseli's art. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 反英雄是一种重要的新型英雄，他建立个人道德准则并追随个人激情以获得自由并满足个人需求。这些价值观将成为富塞利艺术基础的一部分。

- attain: 获得
- antihero: 反英雄
- important new kind: 重要的新型
- hero: 英雄
- established: 建立
- personal moral codes: 个人道德准则
- followed: 追随
- personal passions: 个人激情
- freedom: 自由
- fulfill: 满足
- individual needs: 个人需求
- values: 价值观
- become part of: 成为...的一部分
- foundation: 基础
- Fuseli's art: 富塞利的艺术

2. On the face of it, Plato's argument against democracy seems devastating. If ruling is a skill, and a skill that can only be attained by the few, then democracy seems plainly absurd or irrational. The defender of democracy must find a response to the craft analogy. But does it have a weakness? (Wolff, 2006)

> 从表面上看，柏拉图反对民主的论点似乎是毁灭性的。如果统治是一种技能，而且是一种只能由少数人获得的技能，那么民主似乎显然是荒谬的或不理性的。民主的捍卫者必须找到对工艺类比的回应。但它有弱点吗？

- attain: 获得
- on the face of it: 从表面上看
- Plato's argument: 柏拉图的论点
- against democracy: 反对民主
- seems: 似乎
- devastating: 毁灭性的
- if: 如果
- ruling: 统治
- skill: 技能
- can only be attained: 只能被获得
- by the few: 由少数人
- then: 那么
- plainly: 显然地
- absurd: 荒谬的
- irrational: 不理性的
- defender: 捍卫者
- must find: 必须找到
- response: 回应
- craft analogy: 工艺类比
- but: 但
- weakness: 弱点

3. A person may feel she has thrown away the opportunities that life had to offer, or she would never attain goals cherished since childhood. Yet growing older need not lead to resignation or bleak despair; a release from childhood dreams can be liberating. (Giddens, 2009)

> 一个人可能觉得她浪费了生活必须提供的机会，或者她永远无法实现自童年以来珍视的目标。然而，变老不一定导致放弃或绝望；从童年梦想中解脱可能是解放的。

- attain: 实现
- person: 人
- may feel: 可能觉得
- thrown away: 浪费
- opportunities: 机会
- life had to offer: 生活必须提供的
- would never: 永远无法
- goals: 目标
- cherished: 珍视的
- since childhood: 自童年以来
- yet: 然而
- growing older: 变老
- need not lead to: 不一定导致
- resignation: 放弃
- bleak despair: 绝望
- release: 解脱
- childhood dreams: 童年梦想
- liberating: 解放的

- only by: 只有通过
- trusting: 信任
- their own feelings: 他们自己的感觉
- individuals: 个人
- creative potential: 创造潜力
- achieve: 达到
- self-realization: 自我实现
