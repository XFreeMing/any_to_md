## 应用

1. Fresco is a technique for applying paint to walls that results in an image that is both durable and brilliant. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 湿壁画是一种将颜料涂在墙上的技术，能产生既耐用又明亮的图像。

- apply: 应用，涂
- technique: 技术
- paint: 颜料
- walls: 墙壁
- results in: 产生
- image: 图像
- durable: 耐用的
- brilliant: 明亮的

2. The laboratory allows us to test and revise theories under controlled conditions. Real-world events inspire ideas and provide the venue for applying our theories. (Myers, 2012)

> 实验室允许我们在受控条件下测试和修正理论。现实世界的事件激发想法，并为应用我们的理论提供场所。

- apply: 应用
- laboratory: 实验室
- test: 测试
- revise: 修正
- theories: 理论
- controlled conditions: 受控条件
- real-world events: 现实世界的事件
- inspire: 激发
- ideas: 想法
- provide: 提供
- venue: 场所

3. We cannot even describe social life accurately unless we first grasp the concepts that people apply in their own behaviour. For instance, to describe a death as a 'suicide' means knowing what the person in question was intending when he died.

> 除非我们首先掌握人们在自身行为中应用的概念，否则我们甚至无法准确描述社会生活。例如，将死亡描述为"自杀"意味着知道当事人在死亡时的意图。

- apply: 应用
- describe: 描述
- social life: 社会生活
- accurately: 准确地
- unless: 除非
- grasp: 掌握
- concepts: 概念
- behaviour: 行为
- for instance: 例如
- suicide: 自杀
- means: 意味着
- intending: 意图

They may, for example, get to interact with lecturers and administrators, who then will support them when they are looking for a job or applying for postgraduate courses. (Giddens, 2009)
