1. Another socializing agency is the peer group. Peer groups consist of children of a similar age. In some cultures, particularly small traditional societies, peer groups are formalized as age-grades (normally confined to males). (Giddens, 2009)

> 另一个社会化机构是同龄群体。同龄群体由年龄相似的儿童组成。在一些文化中，特别是小型传统社会，同龄群体被正式化为年龄等级（通常仅限于男性）。

- formalize: 正式化
- another socializing agency: 另一个社会化机构
- peer group: 同龄群体
- consist of: 由...组成
- children: 儿童
- similar age: 相似年龄
- some cultures: 一些文化
- particularly: 特别是
- small traditional societies: 小型传统社会
- age-grades: 年龄等级
- normally confined to: 通常仅限于
- males: 男性

2. Apartheid was encoded in law, but enforced through violence and brutality. The National Party, which formalized apartheid in 1948, used law enforcement and security services to suppress all resistance to the new regime. (Giddens, 2009)

> 种族隔离被编入法律，但通过暴力和野蛮来执行。在1948年将种族隔离正式化的国民党，使用执法和安全服务来镇压对新政权的所有抵抗。

- formalize: 正式化
- apartheid: 种族隔离
- encoded in law: 编入法律
- enforced through: 通过...执行
- violence: 暴力
- brutality: 野蛮
- National Party: 国民党
- 1948: 1948年
- used: 使用
- law enforcement: 执法
- security services: 安全服务
- suppress: 镇压
- all resistance: 所有抵抗
- new regime: 新政权

3. In an influential treatise on painting, Poussin formalized the rules that would govern academic art for centuries. (Fiero, 2011d)

> 在一篇有影响力的绘画论文中，普桑正式化了将统治学术艺术几个世纪的规则。

- formalize: 正式化
- influential treatise: 有影响力的论文
- painting: 绘画
- Poussin: 普桑
- rules: 规则
- govern: 统治
- academic art: 学术艺术
- for centuries: 几个世纪
