1. The sight of the little madeleine had recalled nothing to my mind before I tasted it; perhaps because I had so often seen such things in the interval, without tasting them, on the trays in pastry-cooks' windows, that their image had dissociated itself from those Combray days to take its place among others more recent. (Fiero, 2011)

> 小玛德琳蛋糕的视觉在我品尝它之前没有唤起我脑海中的任何东西；也许是因为我在间隔中经常看到这样的东西，没有品尝它们，在糕点店橱窗的托盘上，它们的形象已经与那些贡布雷的日子分离，在其他更近期的形象中占据一席之地。

- dissociate: 分离
- sight: 视觉
- little madeleine: 小玛德琳蛋糕
- recalled nothing: 没有唤起任何东西
- mind: 脑海
- before I tasted it: 在我品尝它之前
- perhaps because: 也许是因为
- so often seen: 经常看到
- such things: 这样的东西
- interval: 间隔
- without tasting them: 没有品尝它们
- trays: 托盘
- pastry-cooks' windows: 糕点店橱窗
- image: 形象
- Combray days: 贡布雷的日子
- take its place: 占据一席之地
- among others more recent: 在其他更近期的形象中

- dissociate: 分离
- if: 如果
- credible person's message: 可信的人的信息
- persuasive: 有说服力的
- impact: 影响
- may fade: 可能会消失
- source: 来源
- forgotten: 被遗忘
- message: 信息

3. Among these forms of treatment I would like to include our tendency to associate with the people whom we praise and dissociate from the people whom we blame since, for social creatures like us, to be the target of these forms of treatment is desirable and undesirable, respectively. (LaFollette & Persson, 2013)

> 在这些形式的对待中，我想包括我们与我们所赞扬的人交往和与我们责备的人分离的倾向，因为对我们这样的社会生物来说，成为这些形式的对待的目标分别是可取的和不可取的。

- dissociate: 分离
- among: 在...中
- forms of treatment: 形式的对待
- include: 包括
- tendency: 倾向
- associate with: 与...交往
- people: 人们
- praise: 赞扬
- blame: 责备
- since: 因为
- social creatures: 社会生物
- target: 目标
- desirable: 可取的
- undesirable: 不可取的
- respectively: 分别
