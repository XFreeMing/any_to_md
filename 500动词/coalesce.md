1. One group of Indo-Europeans who moved into Asia Minor and Anatolia (modern Turkey) around 1750 B.C. coalesced with the native peoples to form the Hittite kingdom with its capital at Hattusha (Boğazköy in modern Turkey). (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 一群在公元前1750年左右移入小亚细亚和安纳托利亚（现代土耳其）的印欧人与土著民族融合，形成了以哈图沙（现代土耳其的博阿兹柯伊）为首都的赫梯王国。

- coalesce: 融合
- one group: 一群
- Indo-Europeans: 印欧人
- moved into: 移入
- Asia Minor: 小亚细亚
- Anatolia: 安纳托利亚
- modern Turkey: 现代土耳其
- around 1750 B.C.: 公元前1750年左右
- native peoples: 土著民族
- form: 形成
- Hittite kingdom: 赫梯王国
- capital: 首都
- Hattusha: 哈图沙
- Boğazköy: 博阿兹柯伊

2. In 1900, representatives of the trade unions and Fabian Socialists coalesced to form the Labour Party. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 1900年，工会代表和费边社会主义者融合形成了工党。

- coalesce: 融合
- 1900: 1900年
- representatives: 代表
- trade unions: 工会
- Fabian Socialists: 费边社会主义者
- form: 形成
- Labour Party: 工党

3. In the course of the nineteenth century, aristocrats coalesced with the most successful industrialists, bankers, and merchants (the wealthy upper middle class) to form this new elite. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在19世纪的过程中，贵族与最成功的实业家、银行家和商人（富裕的上层中产阶级）融合形成了这个新精英。

- coalesce: 融合
- in the course of: 在...的过程中
- nineteenth century: 19世纪
- aristocrats: 贵族
- most successful: 最成功的
- industrialists: 实业家
- bankers: 银行家
- merchants: 商人
- wealthy upper middle class: 富裕的上层中产阶级
- form: 形成
- new elite: 新精英

4. The macho lads had coalesced as a group by the time they became teenagers; the group's members were in the bottom two academic 'sets' for all subjects.

> 这些大男子主义的男孩在他们成为青少年时已经融合成一个群体；该群体的成员在所有科目的学术"组"中都在倒数两名。

- coalesce: 融合
- macho lads: 大男子主义的男孩
- as a group: 作为一个群体
- by the time: 到...时
- became teenagers: 成为青少年
- group's members: 群体的成员
- bottom two: 倒数两名
- academic sets: 学术组
- all subjects: 所有科目
