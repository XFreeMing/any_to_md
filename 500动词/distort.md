1. Italian Mannerist painters deliberately distorted the rules of proportion by portraying elongated figures that conveyed a sense of suffering and a strong emotional atmosphere filled with anxiety and confusion. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 意大利风格主义画家故意扭曲比例规则，通过描绘拉长的人物来传达一种痛苦感和充满焦虑和困惑的强烈情感氛围。

- distorted: 扭曲
- Italian Mannerist painters: 意大利风格主义画家
- deliberately: 故意地
- rules of proportion: 比例规则
- by portraying: 通过描绘
- elongated figures: 拉长的人物
- conveyed: 传达
- sense of suffering: 痛苦感
- strong emotional atmosphere: 强烈情感氛围
- filled with: 充满
- anxiety: 焦虑
- confusion: 困惑

2. Special effects, achieved by way of computers, are used to juxtapose images in ways that distort reality. (Fiero, 2011)

> 通过计算机实现的特殊效果被用来以扭曲现实的方式并置图像。

- distort: 扭曲
- special effects: 特殊效果
- achieved by way of: 通过...实现
- computers: 计算机
- used to: 被用来
- juxtapose: 并置
- images: 图像
- in ways that: 以...的方式
- reality: 现实

3. On the contrary, all perceptions as well of the sense as of the mind are according to the measure of the individual and not according to the measure of the universe. And the human understanding is like a false mirror, which, receiving rays irregularly, distorts and discolors the nature of things by mingling its own nature with it. (Fiero, 2011)

> 相反，所有感知，无论是感官的还是心灵的，都是根据个人的尺度，而不是根据宇宙的尺度。人类的理解就像一面虚假的镜子，它不规则地接收光线，通过将自己的本质与事物的本质混合来扭曲和变色事物的本质。

- distorts: 扭曲
- on the contrary: 相反
- all perceptions: 所有感知
- as well of the sense as of the mind: 无论是感官的还是心灵的
- according to: 根据
- measure of the individual: 个人的尺度
- measure of the universe: 宇宙的尺度
- human understanding: 人类的理解
- false mirror: 虚假的镜子
- receiving rays irregularly: 不规则地接收光线
- discolors: 变色
- nature of things: 事物的本质
- by mingling: 通过混合
- its own nature: 自己的本质

4. An unjust law is a code that is out of harmony with the moral law. Any law that degrades human personality is unjust. All segregation statutes are unjust because segregation distorts the soul and damages the personality. (Fiero, 2011)

> 不公正的法律是与道德法律不协调的法规。任何贬低人格的法律都是不公正的。所有隔离法规都是不公正的，因为隔离扭曲了灵魂并损害了人格。

- distorts: 扭曲
- unjust law: 不公正的法律
- code: 法规
- out of harmony with: 与...不协调
- moral law: 道德法律
- degrades: 贬低
- human personality: 人格
- segregation statutes: 隔离法规
- segregation: 隔离
- soul: 灵魂
- damages: 损害
- personality: 人格
