1. These electronic networks now have the unprecedented ability to respond immediately to events as they occur, to access and share sources of information, and to put pressure on corporations, governments and international bodies as part of their campaigning strategies. (Giddens, 2009)

> 这些电子网络现在具有前所未有的能力，可以立即响应发生的事件，访问和共享信息来源，并对公司、政府和国际机构施加压力，作为其竞选策略的一部分。

- access: 访问
- electronic networks: 电子网络
- unprecedented ability: 前所未有的能力
- respond immediately: 立即响应
- events: 事件
- as they occur: 当它们发生时
- share: 共享
- sources of information: 信息来源
- put pressure on: 对...施加压力
- corporations: 公司
- governments: 政府
- international bodies: 国际机构
- campaigning strategies: 竞选策略

2. Although the telecommunications infrastructure is not evenly developed around the world, a growing number of countries can now access international communications networks in a way that was previously impossible; over the past decade or so, Internet usage has been growing fastest in those areas that previously lagged behind - Africa and the Middle East for example. (Giddens, 2009)

> 虽然电信基础设施在世界各地发展不均，但越来越多的国家现在可以以前不可能的方式访问国际通信网络；在过去十年左右，互联网使用在以前落后的地区增长最快——例如非洲和中东。

- access: 访问
- although: 虽然
- telecommunications infrastructure: 电信基础设施
- evenly developed: 均匀发展
- around the world: 世界各地
- growing number: 越来越多的
- countries: 国家
- international communications networks: 国际通信网络
- way: 方式
- previously impossible: 以前不可能的
- over the past decade: 在过去十年
- Internet usage: 互联网使用
- growing fastest: 增长最快
- areas: 地区
- previously lagged behind: 以前落后的
- Africa: 非洲
- Middle East: 中东

3. Students in Brisbane, for example, can access online libraries in San Francisco, email specialized academic staff elsewhere to have questions clarified, and collaborate on research projects. (Giddens, 2009)

> 例如，布里斯班的学生可以访问旧金山的在线图书馆，向其他地方的专业学术人员发送电子邮件以澄清问题，并在研究项目上进行合作。

- access: 访问
- students: 学生
- Brisbane: 布里斯班
- for example: 例如
- online libraries: 在线图书馆
- San Francisco: 旧金山
- email: 发送电子邮件
- specialized academic staff: 专业学术人员
- elsewhere: 其他地方
- have questions clarified: 澄清问题
- collaborate: 合作
- research projects: 研究项目

- access: 访问

- allowed us: 使我们能够
