1. In the 1820s, landscape painting began to acquire status and, by the 1840s, it had eclipsed portraiture as the most esteemed form of American art. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在19世纪20年代，风景画开始获得地位，到19世纪40年代，它已经超越了肖像画，成为最受尊敬的美国艺术形式。

- acquire: 获得
- 1820s: 19世纪20年代
- landscape painting: 风景画
- began to: 开始
- status: 地位
- by the 1840s: 到19世纪40年代
- eclipsed: 超越
- portraiture: 肖像画
- most esteemed form: 最受尊敬的形式
- American art: 美国艺术

2. Comparing the extremes of loved children and abused children suggests that parenting does matter. Moreover, children do acquire many of their values, including their political affiliation and religious faith, at home. (Myers, 2012)

> 比较被爱的孩子和被虐待的孩子的极端情况表明，养育确实很重要。此外，孩子们确实在家里获得许多他们的价值观，包括他们的政治归属和宗教信仰。

- acquire: 获得
- comparing: 比较
- extremes: 极端情况
- loved children: 被爱的孩子
- abused children: 被虐待的孩子
- suggests: 表明
- parenting: 养育
- does matter: 确实很重要
- moreover: 此外
- children: 孩子们
- values: 价值观
- including: 包括
- political affiliation: 政治归属
- religious faith: 宗教信仰
- at home: 在家里

3. Introduced into Europe by the Muslims, who acquired it from the Chinese, gunpowder was first used in Western combat during the Hundred Years' War. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 由穆斯林引入欧洲，他们从中国人那里获得了它，火药在百年战争期间首次在西方战斗中使用。

- acquire: 获得
- introduced into: 引入
- Europe: 欧洲
- Muslims: 穆斯林
- Chinese: 中国人
- gunpowder: 火药
- first used: 首次使用
- Western combat: 西方战斗
- during: 在...期间
- Hundred Years' War: 百年战争

4. Gladiatorial games increased dramatically in the Early Empire, and upper-class women acquired greater independence. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 角斗游戏在早期帝国中急剧增加，上层阶级的妇女获得了更大的独立性。

- acquire: 获得
- gladiatorial games: 角斗游戏
- increased dramatically: 急剧增加
- Early Empire: 早期帝国
- upper-class women: 上层阶级的妇女
- greater independence: 更大的独立性
