1. European liberals championed laissez faire because they feared that state interference in the economy to redress social evils would threaten individual rights and the free market, which they thought were essential to personal liberty.  (Wolff, 2006)

> 欧洲自由主义者拥护自由放任，因为他们担心国家干预经济以纠正社会邪恶会威胁个人权利和自由市场，他们认为这些对个人自由至关重要。

- redress: 纠正
- European liberals: 欧洲自由主义者
- championed: 拥护
- laissez faire: 自由放任
- feared: 担心
- state interference: 国家干预
- economy: 经济
- social evils: 社会邪恶
- threaten: 威胁
- individual rights: 个人权利
- free market: 自由市场
- thought: 认为
- essential to: 对...至关重要
- personal liberty: 个人自由

2. Child care subsidies, a minimum hourly wage and guaranteed income levels for families are examples of policy measures that have sought to redress persistent social inequalities. (Giddens, 2009)

> 儿童保育补贴、最低小时工资和家庭保障收入水平是试图纠正持续社会不平等的政策措施的例子。

- redress: 纠正
- child care subsidies: 儿童保育补贴
- minimum hourly wage: 最低小时工资
- guaranteed income levels: 保障收入水平
- families: 家庭
- examples: 例子
- policy measures: 政策措施
- sought to: 试图
- persistent social inequalities: 持续社会不平等

3. Economic restructuring and the loss of industrial workplaces and well-paid jobs has served to produce a more divided society and perceptions that 'nothing can be done' to redress the issue of poverty. (Giddens, 2009)

> 经济重组以及工业工作场所和高薪工作的丧失已经产生了更加分裂的社会和"无法做任何事情"来纠正贫困问题的看法。

- redress: 纠正
- economic restructuring: 经济重组
- loss: 丧失
- industrial workplaces: 工业工作场所
- well-paid jobs: 高薪工作
- served to produce: 产生了
- more divided society: 更加分裂的社会
- perceptions: 看法
- nothing can be done: 无法做任何事情
- issue of poverty: 贫困问题

4. Liberals feared that state intervention in the economy to redress social ills would disrupt the free market, threatening personal liberty and hindering social well-being. (McConnell, Brue, & Flynn, 2014)

> 自由主义者担心国家干预经济以纠正社会弊病会破坏自由市场，威胁个人自由并阻碍社会福祉。

- redress: 纠正
- liberals: 自由主义者
- feared: 担心
- state intervention: 国家干预
- economy: 经济
- social ills: 社会弊病
- disrupt: 破坏
- free market: 自由市场
- threatening: 威胁
- personal liberty: 个人自由
- hindering: 阻碍
- social well-being: 社会福祉
