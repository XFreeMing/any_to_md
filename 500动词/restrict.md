1. The nature of all other creatures is defined and restricted within laws which We have laid down; you, by contrast, impeded by no such restrictions, may, by your own free will, to whose custody. We have assigned you, trace for yourself the lineaments of your 80 own nature. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 所有其他生物的本性都在我们制定的法律范围内被定义和限制；相比之下，你，不受任何此类限制的阻碍，可以凭自己的自由意志，在我们指派给你的监护下，为自己描绘你本性的轮廓。

- restrict: 限制

- nature: 本性

- creatures: 生物

- defined: 定义

- within laws: 在法律范围内

- laid down: 制定

- by contrast: 相比之下

- impeded: 阻碍

- restrictions: 限制

- free will: 自由意志

- custody: 监护

- assigned: 指派

- trace: 描绘

- lineaments: 轮廓

  

2. The new cities were not autonomous entities and soon found themselves dependent on the power of the Hellenistic monarchies. Although the kings did not rule the cities directly, they restricted freedom in other ways. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 新城市不是自治实体，很快就发现自己依赖于希腊化君主制的权力。尽管国王不直接统治这些城市，但他们以其他方式限制了自由。

- restrict: 限制

- new cities: 新城市

- autonomous entities: 自治实体

- dependent on: 依赖于

- power: 权力

- Hellenistic monarchies: 希腊化君主制

- rule: 统治

- directly: 直接地

- freedom: 自由

- other ways: 其他方式

- Bourgeois: 资产阶级的

- merchants: 商人

- manufacturers: 制造商

- responded to: 应对

- decline: 下降

- trade: 贸易

- production: 生产

- attempts: 试图

- competition: 竞争

- resist: 抵制

- demands: 要求

- lower classes: 下层阶级
