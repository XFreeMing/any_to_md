1. In industry and politics men have a great many more positions and they monopolize the most important posts. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 在工业和政治中，男性拥有更多的职位，他们垄断了最重要的职位。

- monopolize: 垄断
- industry: 工业
- politics: 政治
- men: 男性
- great many more positions: 更多的职位
- most important posts: 最重要的职位

2. Many members of the bourgeoisie sought security and status through the purchase of land. They had their own set of grievances because they were often excluded from the social and political privileges monopolized by nobles. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 许多资产阶级成员通过购买土地寻求安全和地位。他们有自己的不满，因为他们经常被排除在由贵族垄断的社会和政治特权之外。

- monopolize: 垄断
- many members: 许多成员
- bourgeoisie: 资产阶级
- sought: 寻求
- security: 安全
- status: 地位
- through: 通过
- purchase of land: 购买土地
- their own set of grievances: 他们自己的不满
- often excluded from: 经常被排除在...之外
- social privileges: 社会特权
- political privileges: 政治特权
- nobles: 贵族

3. Marx did not mean that all inequalities between individuals would disappear. Rather, society would no longer be split into a small class that monopolizes economic and political power and the large mass of people who benefit little from the wealth their work creates. (Giddens, 2009)

> 马克思的意思并不是个人之间的所有不平等都会消失。相反，社会将不再分裂为一个垄断经济和政治权力的小阶级和从他们工作创造的财富中受益很少的大众。

- monopolize: 垄断
- Marx: 马克思
- did not mean: 并不是说
- all inequalities: 所有不平等
- individuals: 个人
- disappear: 消失
- rather: 相反
- society: 社会
- no longer: 不再
- split into: 分裂为
- small class: 小阶级
- economic power: 经济权力
- political power: 政治权力
- large mass of people: 大众
- benefit little from: 从...中受益很少
- wealth: 财富
- work creates: 工作创造的

4. One common objection to such a claim is that it is perfectly obvious in practice that no actual state can live up to the ideal. No state can really monopolize violence, nor can it protect everyone within its territory. (Wolff, 2006)

> 对这种主张的一个常见反对意见是，在实践中很明显，没有哪个实际国家能够达到理想。没有哪个国家能够真正垄断暴力，也不能保护其领土内的每个人。

- monopolize: 垄断
- common objection: 常见反对意见
- such a claim: 这种主张
- perfectly obvious: 很明显
- in practice: 在实践中
- no actual state: 没有哪个实际国家
- live up to: 达到
- ideal: 理想
- really: 真正地
- violence: 暴力
- nor: 也不
- protect: 保护
- everyone: 每个人
- within its territory: 在其领土内
