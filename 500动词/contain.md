1. In putting forth the proposition that an argument must be consistent and contain no contradictions, Parmenides became the founder of formal logic. (Perry, 2010)

> 在提出论证必须一致且不包含矛盾的命题时，巴门尼德成为了形式逻辑的创始人。

- contain: 包含
- putting forth: 提出
- proposition: 命题
- argument: 论证
- consistent: 一致的
- contradictions: 矛盾
- Parmenides: 巴门尼德
- founder: 创始人
- formal logic: 形式逻辑

2. Video pictures resemble photographs in the amount of detail they contain, but, like computer graphics, a video image consists of a series of points of light on a grid, giving the impression of soft focus. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 视频图像在它们包含的细节数量上类似于照片，但就像计算机图形一样，视频图像由网格上的一系列光点组成，给人一种柔焦的印象。

- contain: 包含
- video pictures: 视频图像
- resemble: 类似于
- photographs: 照片
- amount of detail: 细节数量
- computer graphics: 计算机图形
- consists of: 由...组成
- series of points: 一系列点
- light: 光
- grid: 网格
- giving the impression: 给人一种印象
- soft focus: 柔焦

3. Living organisms contain radioactive isotopes (such as carbon-14) that decay at a known rate after its death. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 生物体含有放射性同位素（如碳-14），这些同位素在生物体死亡后以已知的速率衰变。

- contain: 含有
- living organisms: 生物体
- radioactive isotopes: 放射性同位素
- carbon-14: 碳-14
- decay: 衰变
- known rate: 已知的速率
- after its death: 在死亡后

4. The elders, relatively small compared with the other figures, and many of them gesticulating, can barely contain their excitement in the face of the remarkable vision.

> 长者们，相对于其他人物来说较小，其中许多人都在做手势，几乎无法抑制他们在面对这一非凡景象时的兴奋。

- contain: 抑制，控制
- elders: 长者们
- relatively: 相对地
- small: 小的
- compared with: 与...相比
- other figures: 其他人物
- gesticulating: 做手势
- barely: 几乎不
- excitement: 兴奋
- in the face of: 面对
- remarkable: 非凡的
- vision: 景象
