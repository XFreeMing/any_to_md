- disciplined existence: 纪律生活

2. The Renaissance art world was decidedly male-dominated. Few women could become professional artists because of the obstacles they faced. In particular, for centuries, art-training practices mandating residence at a master's house precluded women from acquiring the necessary experience. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 文艺复兴时期的艺术世界绝对是男性主导的。很少有女性能够成为专业艺术家，因为她们面临的障碍。特别是，几个世纪以来，要求在大师家中居住的艺术培训实践阻止了女性获得必要的经验。

- Renaissance: 文艺复兴
- art world: 艺术世界
- decidedly: 绝对地
- male-dominated: 男性主导的
- few women: 很少有女性
- become: 成为
- professional artists: 专业艺术家
- because of: 因为
- obstacles: 障碍
- faced: 面临
- in particular: 特别是
- for centuries: 几个世纪以来
- art-training practices: 艺术培训实践
- mandating: 要求
- residence: 居住
- master's house: 大师的家
- acquiring: 获得
- necessary experience: 必要的经验
