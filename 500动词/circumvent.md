1. Just prior to World War II, imaginative military planners recognized that planes and tanks, properly deployed, could penetrate and smash the enemy's defenses, circumventing the stalemate of trench warfare. (Perry, 2010)

> 就在第二次世界大战之前，富有想象力的军事规划者认识到，适当部署的飞机和坦克可以穿透并粉碎敌人的防御，绕过堑壕战的僵局。

- circumvent: 绕过
- just prior to: 就在...之前
- World War II: 第二次世界大战
- imaginative: 富有想象力的
- military planners: 军事规划者
- recognized: 认识到
- planes: 飞机
- tanks: 坦克
- properly deployed: 适当部署
- penetrate: 穿透
- smash: 粉碎
- enemy's defenses: 敌人的防御
- stalemate: 僵局
- trench warfare: 堑壕战

2. But humans did make attempts to circumvent or relieve their anxiety by discovering the intentions of the gods; these efforts gave rise to the development of the arts of divination. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 但人类确实试图通过发现神的意图来规避或缓解他们的焦虑；这些努力导致了占卜艺术的发展。

- circumvent: 规避
- but: 但
- humans: 人类
- did make attempts: 确实试图
- relieve: 缓解
- anxiety: 焦虑
- discovering: 发现
- intentions: 意图
- gods: 神
- efforts: 努力
- gave rise to: 导致
- development: 发展
- arts of divination: 占卜艺术

3. By the late Republic, however, although the rights of male guardians remained legally in effect, upper-class women found numerous ways to circumvent the power of their guardians.

> 然而，到了共和国晚期，尽管男性监护人的权利在法律上仍然有效，上层阶级的妇女找到了许多方法来规避她们监护人的权力。

- circumvent: 规避
- by the late Republic: 到了共和国晚期
- however: 然而
- although: 尽管
- rights: 权利
- male guardians: 男性监护人
- remained: 仍然
- legally in effect: 在法律上有效
- upper-class women: 上层阶级的妇女
- found: 找到
- numerous ways: 许多方法
- power: 权力
- guardians: 监护人
