1. Therefore, when need requires, and the Pope is a cause of offence to Christendom, in these cases whoever can best do so, as a faithful member of the whole body, must do what he can to procure a true free council. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 因此，当需要要求时，当教皇是基督教世界的冒犯原因时，在这些情况下，任何能够最好地这样做的人，作为整个身体的忠实成员，必须尽其所能获得一个真正的自由会议。

- procure: 获得
- therefore: 因此
- when need requires: 当需要要求时
- Pope: 教皇
- cause of offence: 冒犯原因
- Christendom: 基督教世界
- in these cases: 在这些情况下
- whoever: 任何人
- can best do so: 能够最好地这样做
- faithful member: 忠实成员
- whole body: 整个身体
- must do: 必须做
- what he can: 尽其所能
- true free council: 真正的自由会议

3. The struggle for existence is, in the life of Nature, the basis of all healthy development ... The law of the stronger holds good everywhere. Those forms survive which are able to procure themselves the most favourable conditions of life, and to assert themselves in the universal economy of Nature. (Perry, 2010)

> 生存斗争是，在自然生命中，所有健康发展的基础...强者法则在任何地方都适用。那些能够为自己获得最有利的生活条件并在自然的普遍经济中维护自己的形式得以生存。

- procure: 获得
- struggle for existence: 生存斗争
- life of Nature: 自然生命
- basis: 基础
- healthy development: 健康发展
- law of the stronger: 强者法则

- everywhere: 任何地方
- forms: 形式
- survive: 生存
- able to: 能够
- most favourable conditions: 最有利的条件
- life: 生活

- universal economy: 普遍经济
- Nature: 自然

4. To his contemporaries, Cyrus the Great was deserving of his epithet. The Greek historian Herodotus recounted that the Persians viewed him as a ''father,'' a ruler who was ''gentle, and procured them all manner of goods. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 对他的同时代人来说，居鲁士大帝配得上他的称号。希腊历史学家希罗多德叙述说，波斯人将他视为"父亲"，一个"温和"的统治者，并为他们获得了各种商品。
