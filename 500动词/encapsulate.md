1. However, some readers will object that a particular—and controversial—assumption lies behind both the selection of problems and the positions on them taken here. That assumption is variously called 'individualism', 'atomism', or 'liberal individualism'; often encapsulated by the rather obscure slogan that 'the individual is prior to society'. (Wolff, 2006)

> 然而，一些读者会反对说，在问题选择和这里采取的立场背后有一个特定的——且有争议的——假设。这个假设被不同地称为"个人主义"、"原子论"或"自由个人主义"；通常被一个相当模糊的口号所概括，即"个人先于社会"。

- however: 然而

- readers: 读者

- object: 反对

- particular: 特定的

- controversial: 有争议的

- assumption: 假设

- lies behind: 在...背后

- selection: 选择

- problems: 问题

- positions: 立场

- variously called: 被不同地称为

- individualism: 个人主义

- atomism: 原子论

- liberal individualism: 自由个人主义

- obscure slogan: 模糊的口号

- prior to: 先于
