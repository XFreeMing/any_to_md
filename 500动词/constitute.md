1. Husband, wife, and children constituted the nuclear family, although other dependent relatives and slaves often shared the household. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 丈夫、妻子和孩子构成了核心家庭，尽管其他依赖的亲属和奴隶经常共享家庭。

- constituted: 构成
- husband: 丈夫
- wife: 妻子
- children: 孩子
- nuclear family: 核心家庭
- although: 尽管
- other dependent relatives: 其他依赖的亲属
- slaves: 奴隶
- often shared: 经常共享
- household: 家庭

2. The plebeians constituted the considerably larger group of "independent, unprivileged, poorer, and vulnerable men" as well as large non-patrician landowners, less wealthy landholders, craftspeople, merchants, and small farmers. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 平民构成了相当大的"独立的、无特权的、较贫穷的和脆弱的男性"群体，以及大型非贵族地主、较不富裕的土地持有者、手工艺人、商人和小农。

- constituted: 构成
- plebeians: 平民
- considerably larger group: 相当大的群体
- independent: 独立的
- unprivileged: 无特权的
- poorer: 较贫穷的
- vulnerable men: 脆弱的男性
- as well as: 以及
- large non-patrician landowners: 大型非贵族地主
- less wealthy landholders: 较不富裕的土地持有者
- craftspeople: 手工艺人
- merchants: 商人
- small farmers: 小农

3. The nobiles constituted a governing oligarchy that managed, through its landed wealth, system of patronage, and intimidation, to maintain its hold over the magistracies and senate and thus guide the destiny of Rome while running the state in its own interests. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 贵族构成了一个统治寡头，通过其土地财富、庇护制度和恐吓，维持对行政官和元老院的控制，从而在为自己的利益管理国家的同时指导罗马的命运。

- constituted: 构成
- nobiles: 贵族
- governing oligarchy: 统治寡头
- managed: 管理
- through: 通过
- landed wealth: 土地财富
- system of patronage: 庇护制度
- intimidation: 恐吓
- maintain: 维持
- hold over: 对...的控制
- magistracies: 行政官
- senate: 元老院
- thus: 从而
- guide: 指导
- destiny of Rome: 罗马的命运
- running the state: 管理国家
- in its own interests: 为自己的利益

4. The world constituted a single society of equal human beings. (Oxford English Dictionary, 1989)

> 世界构成了一个平等人类的单一社会。

- constituted: 构成
- world: 世界
- single society: 单一社会
- equal human beings: 平等人类
