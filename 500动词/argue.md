## 认为；主张

1. The question of setting religious faith apart from science troubled early modern intellectuals, even as it does many thinkers in our own time. Some might argue that science and religion remain "locked in a death struggle." (Fiero, 2011)

> 将宗教信仰与科学分开的问题困扰着早期现代知识分子，就像它困扰着我们时代的许多思想家一样。有些人可能会认为，科学和宗教仍然"陷入生死斗争"。

- argue: 认为，主张
- question: 问题
- setting...apart: 将...分开
- religious faith: 宗教信仰
- science: 科学
- troubled: 困扰
- early modern intellectuals: 早期现代知识分子
- even as: 就像
- thinkers: 思想家
- our own time: 我们的时代
- might: 可能
- remain: 仍然
- locked in: 陷入
- death struggle: 生死斗争

2. We can use some of his (Humean) arguments to support the claim that we all do, indeed, benefit from the state. Hume's first step is to argue that each of us will profit if we live in a society governed by norms of justice, such as rules governing private property, and personal security. (Wolff, 2006)

> 我们可以使用他（休谟式）的一些论点来支持我们所有人都确实从国家中受益的主张。休谟的第一步是认为，如果我们生活在一个由正义规范（如管理私有财产和个人安全的规则）治理的社会中，我们每个人都会受益。

- argue: 认为，论证
- use: 使用
- arguments: 论点
- support: 支持
- claim: 主张
- indeed: 确实
- benefit from: 从...受益
- state: 国家
- first step: 第一步
- each of us: 我们每个人
- profit: 受益
- live in: 生活在
- society: 社会
- governed by: 由...治理
- norms of justice: 正义规范
- rules: 规则
- governing: 管理
- private property: 私有财产
- personal security: 个人安全

3. Nevertheless, American historians generally argue that while the rich got richer, the poor, as a result of experiencing an increase in their purchasing power, did not get poorer. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 然而，美国历史学家普遍认为，虽然富人变得更富有，但穷人由于购买力的增加，并没有变得更穷。

- argue: 认为
- nevertheless: 然而
- American historians: 美国历史学家
- generally: 普遍地
- while: 虽然
- rich: 富人
- got richer: 变得更富有
- poor: 穷人
- as a result of: 由于
- experiencing: 经历
- increase: 增加
- purchasing power: 购买力
- did not get poorer: 并没有变得更穷

4. The researchers argued for banning the drug and put forward a well-argued case. (Myers, 2012)

> 研究人员主张禁止这种药物，并提出了一个论证充分的案例。

- argue: 主张，论证
- researchers: 研究人员
- argued for: 主张
- banning: 禁止
- drug: 药物
- put forward: 提出
- well-argued: 论证充分的
- case: 案例
