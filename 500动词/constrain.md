1. Nevertheless, our appropriation from nature must be constrained by two conditions—the 'Lockean provisos'—if it is to be justified: we must not take more than we can make use of (the non-wastage proviso); and we must leave 'enough and as good' for others. These two provisos apply not just to the survival argument for property, but to all of Locke's arguments. (Wolff, 2006)

> 然而，如果我们要从自然中获取资源是合理的，它必须受到两个条件的约束——"洛克条件"：我们不得拿超过我们能使用的（非浪费条件）；我们必须为他人留下"足够且同样好的"。这两个条件不仅适用于财产的生存论证，而且适用于洛克的所有论证。

- constrain: 约束，限制

- nevertheless: 然而

- appropriation: 获取

- nature: 自然

- conditions: 条件

- Lockean provisos: 洛克条件

- justified: 合理的

- make use of: 使用

- non-wastage proviso: 非浪费条件

- enough and as good: 足够且同样好的

- apply to: 适用于

- survival argument: 生存论证

- property: 财产

  

2. If an individual or group possesses a right, others are validly constrained from interfering with the exercise of that right. (Beauchamp & Frey, 2014)

> 如果个人或群体拥有权利，其他人就被有效地约束，不得干涉该权利的行使。

- constrain: 约束

- individual: 个人

- group: 群体

- possesses: 拥有

- right: 权利

- validly: 有效地

- interfering with: 干涉

- exercise: 行使

  

3. While the symbolic interactionist perspective can yield many insights into the nature of our actions in the course of day-to day social life, it has been criticized for ignoring the larger issues of power and structure within society and how they serve to constrain individual action. (Giddens, 2009)

> 虽然符号互动主义视角可以在日常社会生活的过程中对我们的行动本质产生许多见解，但它因忽视社会中权力和结构的更大问题以及它们如何约束个人行动而受到批评。

- constrain: 约束

- symbolic interactionist perspective: 符号互动主义视角

- yield: 产生

- insights: 见解

- nature: 本质

- actions: 行动

- day-to-day social life: 日常生活

- criticized: 批评

- ignoring: 忽视

- larger issues: 更大问题

- power: 权力

- structure: 结构

- society: 社会

- individual action: 个人行动

  

4. Social facts can constrain human action in a variety of ways, ranging from outright punishment (in the case of a crime, for example) to social rejection (in the case of unacceptable behaviour) to simple misunderstanding (in the case of the misuse of language).

> 社会事实可以以多种方式约束人类行动，从直接惩罚（例如在犯罪的情况下）到社会排斥（在不可接受行为的情况下）再到简单的误解（在误用语言的情况下）。

- constrain: 约束

- social facts: 社会事实

- human action: 人类行动

- variety of ways: 多种方式

- ranging from: 从...到

- outright punishment: 直接惩罚

- crime: 犯罪

- social rejection: 社会排斥

- unacceptable behaviour: 不可接受行为

- misunderstanding: 误解

- misuse of language: 误用语言
