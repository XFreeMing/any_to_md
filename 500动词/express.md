1. The reality is more complex, but it is fair to say that most Egyptian artists did not strive for innovation or originality, but adhered instead to traditional formulations that expressed specific ideas. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 现实更加复杂，但可以公平地说，大多数埃及艺术家并不追求创新或原创性，而是坚持表达特定思想的传统形式。

- express: 表达
- reality: 现实
- more complex: 更加复杂
- fair to say: 可以公平地说
- most Egyptian artists: 大多数埃及艺术家
- did not strive for: 并不追求
- innovation: 创新
- originality: 原创性
- adhered instead to: 而是坚持
- traditional formulations: 传统形式
- specific ideas: 特定思想

2. There is still great scholarly disagreement about how we ought to read Locke's arguments. Not even Locke could have thought that they are clearly expressed. But there is no doubt that for Locke labour is all-important in the appropriation of property. (Wolff, 2006)

> 关于我们应该如何阅读洛克的论点，仍然存在很大的学术分歧。甚至洛克也不可能认为它们表达得很清楚。但毫无疑问，对洛克来说，劳动在财产的占有中是至关重要的。

- express: 表达
- still: 仍然
- great scholarly disagreement: 很大的学术分歧
- about: 关于
- how: 如何
- ought to read: 应该阅读
- Locke's arguments: 洛克的论点
- not even: 甚至不
- could have thought: 可能认为
- clearly: 清楚地
- but: 但
- no doubt: 毫无疑问
- for Locke: 对洛克来说
- labour: 劳动
- all-important: 至关重要的
- appropriation: 占有
- property: 财产

3. The most enduring literature of the era, however, expressed the bitter anguish of the war experience itself. (Fiero, 2011f)

> 然而，那个时代最持久的文学表达了战争经历本身的痛苦。

- express: 表达
- most enduring: 最持久的
- literature: 文学
- era: 时代
- however: 然而
- bitter anguish: 痛苦
- war experience: 战争经历
- itself: 本身

4. The views expressed by opinion leaders, filtered through personal relationships, influence the responses of other individuals towards political issues of the day. (Giddens, 2009)

> 意见领袖表达的观点，通过个人关系过滤，影响其他个人对当前政治问题的反应。

- express: 表达
- views: 观点
- opinion leaders: 意见领袖
- filtered through: 通过...过滤
- personal relationships: 个人关系
- influence: 影响
- responses: 反应
- other individuals: 其他个人
- towards: 对
- political issues: 政治问题
- of the day: 当前的
