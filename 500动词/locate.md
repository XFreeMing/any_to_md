1. The Haida folk of the Queen Charlotte Islands, located near British Columbia, continue the ancient practice of raising wooden poles carved and painted with totems — heraldic symbols of social status, spiritual authority, and ancestral pride.

> 位于不列颠哥伦比亚省附近的夏洛特皇后群岛的海达族人继续着竖立雕刻和绘制图腾的木杆的古老做法——这些是代表社会地位、精神权威和祖先骄傲的纹章符号。

- locate: 位于

- Haida folk: 海达族人

- Queen Charlotte Islands: 夏洛特皇后群岛

- British Columbia: 不列颠哥伦比亚省

- continue: 继续

- ancient practice: 古老做法

- raising: 竖立

- wooden poles: 木杆

- carved: 雕刻的

- painted: 绘制的

- totems: 图腾

- heraldic symbols: 纹章符号

- social status: 社会地位

- spiritual authority: 精神权威

- ancestral pride: 祖先骄傲
