1. Unlike the Romans, the Greeks did not isolate an individual's personality in portrait busts, but considered it to animate the full body. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 与罗马人不同，希腊人没有在肖像半身像中孤立个人的个性，而是认为它使整个身体充满活力。

- isolate: 孤立
- unlike: 与...不同
- Romans: 罗马人
- Greeks: 希腊人
- did not: 没有
- individual's personality: 个人的个性
- portrait busts: 肖像半身像
- considered: 认为
- animate: 使...充满活力
- full body: 整个身体

2. The same council encouraged the development of Jewish ghettos, neighborhoods built behind walled enclosures to isolate Jews from Christians. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 同一个委员会鼓励发展犹太贫民区，这些社区建在有围墙的围栏后面，以将犹太人与基督徒隔离开来。

- isolate: 隔离
- same council: 同一个委员会
- encouraged: 鼓励
- development: 发展
- Jewish ghettos: 犹太贫民区
- neighborhoods: 社区
- built: 建
- behind: 在...后面
- walled enclosures: 有围墙的围栏
- Jews: 犹太人
- from: 从
- Christians: 基督徒

3. It is clear that even studies of identical twins cannot fully isolate biological from social factors. It is often the case that, even in infancy, identical twins are treated more like one another by parents, peers and teachers than are fraternal twins, who in turn are treated more like one another than are adoptive siblings. (Giddens, 2009)

> 很明显，即使是对同卵双胞胎的研究也无法完全将生物因素与社会因素分离开来。情况往往是，即使在婴儿期，同卵双胞胎被父母、同龄人和老师对待的方式也比异卵双胞胎更相似，而异卵双胞胎又被对待得比收养的兄弟姐妹更相似。

- isolate: 分离
- clear: 清楚的
- even studies: 即使研究
- identical twins: 同卵双胞胎
- cannot fully: 无法完全
- biological: 生物的
- from: 从
- social factors: 社会因素
- often the case: 情况往往是
- even in infancy: 即使在婴儿期
- treated: 被对待
- more like one another: 更相似
- parents: 父母
- peers: 同龄人
- teachers: 老师
- than: 比
- fraternal twins: 异卵双胞胎
- in turn: 反过来
- adoptive siblings: 收养的兄弟姐妹

4. Actual cases vary in so many ways—in the type of crime, in the status, age, gender, and race of the defendant—that it's difficult to isolate the factors that influence jurors.

> 实际案件在如此多的方面有所不同——在犯罪类型、被告的地位、年龄、性别和种族方面——以至于很难分离出影响陪审员的因素。

- isolate: 分离
- actual cases: 实际案件
- vary: 不同
- so many ways: 如此多的方面
- type of crime: 犯罪类型
- status: 地位
- age: 年龄
- gender: 性别
- race: 种族
- defendant: 被告
- difficult: 困难的
- factors: 因素
- influence: 影响
- jurors: 陪审员
