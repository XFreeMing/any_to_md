1. By spring 333, the entire western half of Asia Minor was in Alexander's hands, and the Ionian Greek cities of western Asia Minor had been ''liberated'' from the Persian oppressor. Meanwhile, the Persian king, Darius III, mobilized his forces to stop Alexander's army. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 到公元前333年春天，小亚细亚的整个西半部都在亚历山大的手中，小亚细亚西部的爱奥尼亚希腊城市已经从波斯压迫者手中"解放"出来。与此同时，波斯国王大流士三世动员了他的军队来阻止亚历山大的军队。

- mobilize: 动员
- by spring 333: 到公元前333年春天
- entire western half: 整个西半部
- Asia Minor: 小亚细亚
- Alexander's hands: 亚历山大的手中
- Ionian Greek cities: 爱奥尼亚希腊城市
- liberated: 解放
- Persian oppressor: 波斯压迫者
- meanwhile: 与此同时
- Persian king: 波斯国王
- Darius III: 大流士三世
- forces: 军队
- stop: 阻止
- Alexander's army: 亚历山大的军队

2. Over a period of four years, the Union states of the North mobilized their superior assets and gradually wore down the Confederate forces of the South. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在四年的时间里，北方的联邦州动员了他们的优势资产，并逐渐削弱了南方的邦联军队。

- mobilize: 动员
- over a period of: 在...期间
- four years: 四年
- Union states: 联邦州
- North: 北方
- superior assets: 优势资产
- gradually: 逐渐地
- wore down: 削弱
- Confederate forces: 邦联军队
- South: 南方

3. The First Crusade, mobilized by Pope Urban II in 1095, managed to claim Jerusalem after three years, but later crusades were generally disastrous. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 第一次十字军东征，由教皇乌尔班二世在1095年动员，在三年后成功占领了耶路撒冷，但后来的十字军东征通常是灾难性的。

- mobilize: 动员
- First Crusade: 第一次十字军东征
- Pope Urban II: 教皇乌尔班二世
- 1095: 1095年
- managed to: 成功
- claim: 占领
- Jerusalem: 耶路撒冷
- after three years: 三年后
- later crusades: 后来的十字军东征
- generally: 通常
- disastrous: 灾难性的

4. Insisting on military service from the allies in the Roman Confederation, Rome essentially mobilized the entire military manpower of all Italy for its wars.

> 坚持要求罗马联盟中的盟友提供军事服务，罗马基本上动员了整个意大利的所有军事人力来为其战争服务。

- mobilize: 动员
- insisting on: 坚持要求
- military service: 军事服务
- allies: 盟友
- Roman Confederation: 罗马联盟
- Rome: 罗马
- essentially: 基本上
- entire military manpower: 整个军事人力
- all Italy: 整个意大利
- wars: 战争
