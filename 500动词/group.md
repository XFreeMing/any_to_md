1. While many artists accepted this term, others used different labels, all of which can be grouped under the umbrella term Performance Art, which is distinguished from theater in that it takes place in an art context. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 虽然许多艺术家接受了这个术语，但其他人使用了不同的标签，所有这些都可以归入"行为艺术"这个总称下，它与戏剧的区别在于它发生在艺术语境中。

- group: 归类，分组

- artists: 艺术家

- accepted: 接受

- term: 术语

- used: 使用

- different labels: 不同的标签

- umbrella term: 总称

- Performance Art: 行为艺术

- distinguished from: 与...区别

- theater: 戏剧

- takes place: 发生

- art context: 艺术语境

  

2. Explanations of poverty can be grouped under two main headings: theories that see poor individuals as responsible for their own poverty, and theories that view poverty as produced and reproduced by structural forces in society. (Giddens, 2009)

> 对贫困的解释可以归为两个主要标题：将贫困个人视为对自己贫困负责的理论，以及将贫困视为由社会中的结构性力量产生和再生产的理论。

- group: 归类

- explanations: 解释

- poverty: 贫困

- two main headings: 两个主要标题

- theories: 理论

- poor individuals: 贫困个人

- responsible for: 对...负责

- view: 视为

- produced: 产生

- reproduced: 再生产

- structural forces: 结构性力量

- society: 社会

  

3. Adjoining the church to the south is an arcaded cloister, around which are grouped the monks dormitory (on the east side), a refectory (dining hall) and kitchen (on the south side), and a cellar. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 与教堂南侧相邻的是一个拱廊回廊，周围聚集着僧侣宿舍（在东侧）、食堂和厨房（在南侧）以及地窖。

- group: 聚集，围绕

- adjoining: 相邻的

- church: 教堂

- south: 南侧

- arcaded cloister: 拱廊回廊

- around which: 周围

- monks dormitory: 僧侣宿舍

- east side: 东侧

- refectory: 食堂

- dining hall: 餐厅

- kitchen: 厨房

- south side: 南侧

- cellar: 地窖

  

4. The crew were grouped on the forecastle; the broad stage is run far out over the port bow, and an envied deck-hand stands picturesquely on the end of it with a coil of rope in his hand. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 船员聚集在前甲板上；宽阔的舞台延伸到左舷船首上方很远，一个令人羡慕的甲板水手手里拿着一卷绳子，如画般地站在它的末端。

- group: 聚集

- crew: 船员

- forecastle: 前甲板

- broad stage: 宽阔的舞台

- run far out: 延伸很远

- port bow: 左舷船首

- envied: 令人羡慕的

- deck-hand: 甲板水手

- stands: 站立

- picturesquely: 如画般地

- end: 末端

- coil of rope: 一卷绳子
