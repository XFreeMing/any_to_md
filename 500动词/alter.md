1. The wars altered some boundaries on the European continent but were perhaps more significant for the victories that marked the emergence of Great Britain as the world's greatest naval and colonial power. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 这些战争改变了欧洲大陆的一些边界，但也许更重要的是标志着大不列颠作为世界最强大的海军和殖民强国崛起的胜利。

- altered: 改变
- wars: 战争
- boundaries: 边界
- European continent: 欧洲大陆
- perhaps more significant: 也许更重要
- victories: 胜利
- marked: 标志着
- emergence: 崛起
- Great Britain: 大不列颠
- world's greatest: 世界最强大的
- naval and colonial power: 海军和殖民强国

2. The Catholic church had become one Christian denomination among many with an organizational framework and doctrinal pattern that would not be significantly altered until the Second Vatican Council four hundred years later. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 天主教会已成为众多基督教教派之一，其组织框架和教义模式直到四百年后的第二次梵蒂冈大公会议才被显著改变。

- altered: 改变
- Catholic church: 天主教会
- become: 成为
- one Christian denomination: 一个基督教教派
- among many: 在众多中
- organizational framework: 组织框架
- doctrinal pattern: 教义模式
- would not be significantly: 不会被显著
- until: 直到
- Second Vatican Council: 第二次梵蒂冈大公会议
- four hundred years later: 四百年后

3. The French Revolution dramatically altered the political structure of France; the Napoleonic conquests then spread many of the revolutionary principles to other parts of Europe.

> 法国大革命戏剧性地改变了法国的政治结构；拿破仑的征服随后将许多革命原则传播到欧洲其他地区。

- altered: 改变
- French Revolution: 法国大革命
- dramatically: 戏剧性地
- political structure: 政治结构
- France: 法国
- Napoleonic conquests: 拿破仑的征服
- then: 随后
- spread: 传播
- revolutionary principles: 革命原则
- other parts of Europe: 欧洲其他地区

4. The Hellenistic monarchies provided a sort of stability of several centuries, even though Hellenistic kings refused to accept the new status quo and periodically engaged in wars to alter it. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 希腊化君主制提供了几个世纪的某种稳定，尽管希腊化国王拒绝接受新的现状并定期参与战争来改变它。

- alter: 改变
- Hellenistic monarchies: 希腊化君主制
- provided: 提供
- sort of stability: 某种稳定
- several centuries: 几个世纪
- even though: 尽管
- Hellenistic kings: 希腊化国王
- refused to accept: 拒绝接受
- new status quo: 新的现状
- periodically: 定期地
- engaged in wars: 参与战争
