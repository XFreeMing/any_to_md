1. Predicting people's behavior is like predicting a baseball or cricket player's hitting. The outcome of any particular turn at bat is nearly impossible to predict. But when we aggregate many times at bat, we can compare their approximate batting averages. (Myers, 2012)

> 预测人们的行为就像预测棒球或板球运动员的击球。任何特定击球回合的结果几乎不可能预测。但当我们汇总多次击球时，我们可以比较他们的大致击球率。

- aggregate: 汇总
- predicting: 预测
- people's behavior: 人们的行为
- like: 就像
- baseball player: 棒球运动员
- cricket player: 板球运动员
- hitting: 击球
- outcome: 结果
- particular turn: 特定回合
- at bat: 击球
- nearly impossible: 几乎不可能
- predict: 预测
- many times: 多次
- compare: 比较
- approximate batting averages: 大致击球率

2. The fact that national parties invariably articulate the demands of a multitude of groups forces them to aggregate these interests by drawing them together into a coherent whole, balancing competing interests against each other. (Heywood, 2013)

> 国家政党总是表达众多群体的需求这一事实迫使他们通过将这些利益聚集在一起形成一个连贯的整体来汇总这些利益，平衡相互竞争的利益。

- aggregate: 汇总
- fact: 事实
- national parties: 国家政党
- invariably: 总是
- articulate: 表达
- demands: 需求
- multitude of groups: 众多群体
- forces them to: 迫使他们
- interests: 利益
- drawing them together: 将它们聚集在一起
- coherent whole: 连贯的整体
- balancing: 平衡
- competing interests: 相互竞争的利益
- against each other: 相互

3. This raises the question of whether non-consequentialism requires us to give each person an equal chance to be helped, or permits us to aggregate and help the greater number of people. (LaFollette & Persson, 2013)

> 这提出了一个问题：非结果主义是否要求我们给每个人一个平等的被帮助的机会，或者允许我们汇总并帮助更多的人。

- aggregate: 汇总
- raises the question: 提出问题
- whether: 是否
- non-consequentialism: 非结果主义
- requires us to: 要求我们
- give: 给
- each person: 每个人
- equal chance: 平等机会
- be helped: 被帮助
- permits us to: 允许我们
- help: 帮助
- greater number of people: 更多的人

4. If we have a list of relevant capabilities, we would still be left wondering whether the capabilities should be aggregated and, if so, what their relative weights and the formula to aggregate them will or should be.

> 如果我们有一个相关能力列表，我们仍然会想知道这些能力是否应该被汇总，如果是，它们的相对权重和汇总它们的公式将是什么或应该是什么。

- aggregate: 汇总
- if: 如果
- list: 列表
- relevant capabilities: 相关能力
- still be left wondering: 仍然想知道
- whether: 是否
- capabilities: 能力
- should be aggregated: 应该被汇总
- if so: 如果是
- relative weights: 相对权重
- formula: 公式
- will or should be: 将是什么或应该是什么
