1. If the bronze Zeus suggested impending motion by portraying the moment before it occurred, Myron condensed a sequence of movements into a single pose, achieved through a violent twist of the torso that brings the arms into the same plane as the legs. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 如果青铜宙斯通过描绘运动发生前的瞬间来暗示即将到来的运动，那么米隆将一系列运动浓缩成一个单一的姿势，通过躯干的剧烈扭转实现，使手臂与腿部处于同一平面。

- condense: 浓缩
- bronze Zeus: 青铜宙斯
- suggested: 暗示
- impending motion: 即将到来的运动
- portraying: 描绘
- moment before: ...之前的瞬间
- occurred: 发生
- Myron: 米隆
- sequence of movements: 一系列运动
- single pose: 单一姿势
- achieved through: 通过...实现
- violent twist: 剧烈扭转
- torso: 躯干
- brings: 使
- arms: 手臂
- same plane: 同一平面
- legs: 腿部

- artist: 艺术家
- designed: 设计
- scene: 场景
- Genesis 13: 创世记13章

> 列奥纳多显然想要浓缩他的主题，无论是在物理上（通过紧凑、纪念性的人物组合）还是在精神上（通过同时呈现多个意义层次）。

- condense: 浓缩
- Leonardo: 列奥纳多

- many levels of meaning: 多个意义层次
- at one time: 同时

4. In the second epoch, estimated at about 35,000 years, continued cooling produced further solidification with the formation in the contracting surface of primitive valleys and mountains, all surrounded by an atmosphere as yet too hot for water to condense. (Goodwin, 1976)

> 在第二个时期，估计约35,000年，持续冷却产生了进一步的固化，在收缩表面形成了原始的山谷和山脉，所有这些都被一个仍然太热而无法使水凝结的大气所包围。
