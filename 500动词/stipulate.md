1. Strict laws stipulated that a citizen must be the offspring of a legally acknowledged marriage between two Athenian citizens whose parents were also citizens.

> 严格的法律规定，公民必须是两个雅典公民之间合法承认的婚姻的后代，这两个公民的父母也必须是公民。

- stipulate: 规定
- strict laws: 严格的法律
- citizen: 公民
- must be: 必须是
- offspring: 后代
- legally acknowledged marriage: 合法承认的婚姻
- between: 在...之间
- two Athenian citizens: 两个雅典公民
- whose parents: 他们的父母
- also citizens: 也必须是公民

2. A Frankish church council in 789 stipulated that marriage was ''indissoluble'' and condemned the practice of concubinage and easy divorce, and during the reign of Emperor Louis the Pious (814--840), the church formally prohibited divorce. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 789年的法兰克教会会议规定婚姻是"不可解除的"，并谴责了纳妾和轻易离婚的做法，在虔诚者路易皇帝（814-840）统治期间，教会正式禁止离婚。

- stipulate: 规定
- Frankish church council: 法兰克教会会议
- 789: 789年
- marriage: 婚姻
- indissoluble: 不可解除的
- condemned: 谴责
- practice: 做法
- concubinage: 纳妾
- easy divorce: 轻易离婚
- during: 在...期间
- reign: 统治
- Emperor Louis the Pious: 虔诚者路易皇帝
- 814--840: 814-840年
- church: 教会
- formally prohibited: 正式禁止
- divorce: 离婚

3. The Factory Act of 1833 stipulated that children between nine and thirteen could work only eight hours a day; those between thirteen and eighteen, twelve hours.

> 1833年的《工厂法》规定，9至13岁的儿童每天只能工作8小时；13至18岁的儿童，12小时。

- stipulate: 规定
- Factory Act of 1833: 1833年的《工厂法》
- children: 儿童
- between nine and thirteen: 9至13岁
- could work only: 只能工作
- eight hours a day: 每天8小时
- those between thirteen and eighteen: 13至18岁的儿童
- twelve hours: 12小时
