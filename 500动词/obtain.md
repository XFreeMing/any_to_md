1. Although gold had been obtained in Europe through mining since Roman times, the most common method of acquiring it was by collecting nuggets or small grains from rivers and streams (called placer or alluvial gold). (Davies et al., 2015)

> 虽然黄金自罗马时代以来就在欧洲通过采矿获得，但最常见的获取方法是从河流和溪流中收集金块或小颗粒（称为砂金或冲积金）。

- obtain: 获得
- although: 虽然
- gold: 黄金
- Europe: 欧洲
- through mining: 通过采矿
- since Roman times: 自罗马时代以来
- most common method: 最常见的方法
- acquiring: 获取
- collecting: 收集
- nuggets: 金块
- small grains: 小颗粒
- rivers: 河流
- streams: 溪流
- placer: 砂金
- alluvial gold: 冲积金

2. And what should the relation be between political power and economic success? In some countries few obtain political power unless they are already wealthy. In others, those who gain political power soon find themselves rich. (Wolff, 2006)

> 政治权力和经济成功之间应该是什么关系？在一些国家，很少有人获得政治权力，除非他们已经富有。在另一些国家，那些获得政治权力的人很快发现自己变得富有。

- obtain: 获得
- what should the relation be: 应该是什么关系
- between: 在...之间
- political power: 政治权力
- economic success: 经济成功
- some countries: 一些国家
- few: 很少人
- unless: 除非
- already wealthy: 已经富有
- others: 另一些国家
- those who: 那些...的人
- gain: 获得
- soon: 很快
- find themselves: 发现自己
- rich: 富有

3. Before a serf could marry, he had to obtain the lord's permission and pay a fee. (Perry, 2010)

> 在农奴可以结婚之前，他必须获得领主的许可并支付费用。

- obtain: 获得
- before: 在...之前
- serf: 农奴
- could marry: 可以结婚
- had to: 必须
- lord's permission: 领主的许可
- pay: 支付
- fee: 费用

4. The study concluded that the young people who coped best with the transition to adulthood were those who had obtained a better education and postponed children and marriage. (Giddens, 2009)

> 研究得出结论，最能应对向成年过渡的年轻人是那些获得了更好教育并推迟了孩子和婚姻的人。

- obtain: 获得
- study: 研究
- concluded: 得出结论
- young people: 年轻人
- coped best with: 最能应对
- transition: 过渡
- adulthood: 成年
- better education: 更好教育
- postponed: 推迟
- children: 孩子
- marriage: 婚姻
