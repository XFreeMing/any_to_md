1. The third challenge to the inclusion of rights in an ethical theory is much more interesting than either of the other two. It emanates from relatively recent developments in feminist ethical theory (see, for example, Hardwig 1990; Sherwin 1992). (LaFollette & Persson, 2013)

> 对在伦理理论中包含权利的第三个挑战比另外两个中的任何一个都更有趣。它源于女性主义伦理理论中相对较新的发展（例如，见哈德维格1990；舍温1992）。

- emanate: 源于
- third challenge: 第三个挑战
- inclusion of rights: 包含权利
- ethical theory: 伦理理论
- much more interesting: 更有趣
- either of the other two: 另外两个中的任何一个
- relatively recent developments: 相对较新的发展
- feminist ethical theory: 女性主义伦理理论
- Hardwig 1990: 哈德维格1990
- Sherwin 1992: 舍温1992

2. It is often affirmed that utilitarianism renders men cold and unsympathizing; that it chills their moral feelings towards individuals; that it makes them regard only the dry and hard consideration of the consequences of actions, not taking into their moral estimate the qualities from which those actions emanate. (Shafer-Landau, 2012)

> 人们经常断言，功利主义使人们变得冷漠和不具同情心；它冷却了他们对个人的道德感受；它使他们只考虑行动后果的枯燥和硬性的考虑，而不考虑那些行动所源于的品质的道德评估。

- emanate: 源于
- often affirmed: 经常断言
- utilitarianism: 功利主义
- renders: 使
- men: 人们
- cold: 冷漠的
- unsympathizing: 不具同情心的
- chills: 冷却
- moral feelings: 道德感受
- towards individuals: 对个人
- makes them regard: 使他们考虑
- dry and hard consideration: 枯燥和硬性的考虑
- consequences of actions: 行动后果
- taking into: 考虑
- moral estimate: 道德评估
- qualities: 品质
- those actions: 那些行动

3. Moreover, I fail to see how such a condition could emanate from the social character of morality. (Shafer-Landau, 2012)

> 此外，我无法理解这样的条件如何能够从道德的社会特征中产生。

- emanate: 产生
- moreover: 此外
- fail to see: 无法理解
- how: 如何
- such a condition: 这样的条件
- social character: 社会特征
- morality: 道德

4. The source of all sovereignty is located in essence in the nation; no body, no individual can exercise authority which does not emanate from it expressly. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 所有主权的来源本质上位于国家；任何机构、任何个人都不能行使不是明确从它产生的权威。

- emanate: 产生
- source: 来源
- all sovereignty: 所有主权
- located in essence: 本质上位于
- nation: 国家
- no body: 任何机构
- no individual: 任何个人
- exercise: 行使
- authority: 权威
- expressly: 明确地
