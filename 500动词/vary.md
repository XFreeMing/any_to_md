1. The dating of events can also vary from people to people. Most people in the Western world use the Western calendar, also known as the Gregorian calendar after Pope Gregory XIII, who refined it in 1582. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 事件的日期也可能因人而异。西方世界的大多数人使用西方历法，也称为格里高利历，以教皇格里高利十三世命名，他于1582年对其进行了改进。

- vary: 变化，不同

- dating: 日期确定

- events: 事件

- Western calendar: 西方历法

- Gregorian calendar: 格里高利历

- refined: 改进

  

2. The details of this objection vary from opponent to opponent—conservatives will say that liberal individualism offers much more than is feasible; radicals that it offers much less than is desirable. (Wolff, 2006)

> 这种反对意见的细节因对手而异——保守派会说自由个人主义提供的远超过可行的；激进派会说它提供的远少于理想的。

- vary: 变化，不同

- details: 细节

- objection: 反对意见

- opponent: 对手

- conservatives: 保守派

- liberal individualism: 自由个人主义

- feasible: 可行的

- radicals: 激进派

- desirable: 理想的

  

3. There are several reasons to believe that social factors play a role here, too (Spelke, 2005). First, sex differences in math and science achievement vary across cultures. Second, some domains of sex differences, such as math, have decreased over time. (Helgeson, 2011)

> 有几个理由相信社会因素在这里也起作用。首先，数学和科学成就中的性别差异因文化而异。其次，一些性别差异的领域，如数学，随着时间的推移已经减少。

- vary: 变化，不同

- social factors: 社会因素

- play a role: 起作用

- sex differences: 性别差异

- achievement: 成就

- across cultures: 跨文化

- domains: 领域

- decreased: 减少

  

4. The total number of slaves is difficult to judge---estimates vary from 20 to 30 percent of the population. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 奴隶的总数难以判断——估计在人口的20%到30%之间变化。

- vary: 变化，在...范围内

- total number: 总数

- slaves: 奴隶

- difficult to judge: 难以判断

- estimates: 估计

- percent: 百分比

- population: 人口
