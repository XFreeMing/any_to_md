1. Various techniques aided artists in the task of recreating the illusion of reality. The technique of oil painting, refined by Jan van Eyck, was among the first of these. (Fiero, 2011d)

> 各种技术帮助艺术家完成重现现实幻觉的任务。由扬·凡·艾克完善的油画技术是其中最早的技术之一。

- refine: 完善
- various techniques: 各种技术
- aided: 帮助
- artists: 艺术家
- task: 任务
- recreating: 重现
- illusion of reality: 现实幻觉
- technique of oil painting: 油画技术
- Jan van Eyck: 扬·凡·艾克
- among the first: 最早之一

2. Stone tools became refined as flint blades were employed to make sickles and hoes for use in the fields. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 随着燧石刀片被用来制作用于田间的镰刀和锄头，石器变得精良。

- refine: 精良
- stone tools: 石器
- became: 变得
- flint blades: 燧石刀片
- employed: 被用来
- make: 制作
- sickles: 镰刀
- hoes: 锄头
- use in the fields: 用于田间

3. The global coverage has been refined with revised coverage of the early Americas, new content on archeological discoveries in ancient Peru, a segment on the role of the West in the Islamic Middle East, and a discussion of China's global ascendance. (Fiero, 2011b)

> 全球覆盖已经通过修订的早期美洲覆盖、关于古秘鲁考古发现的新内容、关于西方在伊斯兰中东作用的片段以及关于中国全球崛起的讨论而得到完善。

- refine: 完善
- global coverage: 全球覆盖
- revised coverage: 修订的覆盖
- early Americas: 早期美洲
- new content: 新内容
- archeological discoveries: 考古发现
- ancient Peru: 古秘鲁
- segment: 片段
- role of the West: 西方的作用
- Islamic Middle East: 伊斯兰中东
- discussion: 讨论
- China's global ascendance: 中国的全球崛起

4. A woman may make a man's home delightful, and may thus increase his motives for virtuous exertion. She may refine and tranquilize his mind,---may turn away his anger or allay his grief.

> 一个女人可以使一个男人的家变得愉快，从而增加他进行美德努力的动机。她可以完善和安抚他的心灵——可以转移他的愤怒或减轻他的悲伤。

- refine: 完善
- woman: 女人
- make: 使
- man's home: 男人的家
- delightful: 愉快的
- increase: 增加
- motives: 动机
- virtuous exertion: 美德努力
- tranquilize: 安抚
- mind: 心灵
- turn away: 转移
- anger: 愤怒
- allay: 减轻
- grief: 悲伤
