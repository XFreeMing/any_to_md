1. Aristocratic factions continued to vie for power, and the poorer peasants resented Solon's failure to institute land redistribution. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 贵族派系继续争夺权力，较贫穷的农民对梭伦未能建立土地重新分配感到不满。

- institute: 建立
- aristocratic factions: 贵族派系
- continued to: 继续
- vie for power: 争夺权力
- poorer peasants: 较贫穷的农民
- resented: 对...感到不满
- Solon's failure: 梭伦的失败
- land redistribution: 土地重新分配

2. The patricians, realizing that they could not defend Rome by themselves, were forced to compromise. Two new officials known as tribunes of the plebs were instituted (later raised to five and then ten in number). (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 贵族们意识到他们无法独自保卫罗马，被迫妥协。建立了两个被称为平民保民官的新官员（后来增加到五个，然后是十个）。

- instituted: 建立
- patricians: 贵族
- realizing that: 意识到
- could not defend: 无法保卫
- Rome: 罗马
- by themselves: 独自
- forced to compromise: 被迫妥协
- two new officials: 两个新官员
- known as: 被称为
- tribunes of the plebs: 平民保民官
- later raised to: 后来增加到
- five and then ten in number: 五个，然后是十个

3. A provisional government was soon replaced by the Constituent Assembly. But the working class was still not represented; already organized into labor camps instituted by the new government, it revolted, storming Parliament. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 临时政府很快被制宪会议取代。但工人阶级仍然没有代表；已经被组织到新政府建立的劳动营中，它反叛了，冲击了议会。

- instituted: 建立
- provisional government: 临时政府
- soon replaced by: 很快被...取代
- Constituent Assembly: 制宪会议
- working class: 工人阶级
- still not represented: 仍然没有代表
- already organized into: 已经被组织到
- labor camps: 劳动营
- new government: 新政府
- revolted: 反叛
- storming Parliament: 冲击议会

4. Philip instituted military reforms that transformed Macedonia into a major military power. He created a new phalanx of infantrymen who were more lightly armed than Greek hoplites; each carried a smaller shield and a shorter sword.

> 菲利普建立了军事改革，将马其顿转变为一个主要军事强国。他创建了一个新的步兵方阵，这些步兵比希腊重装步兵装备更轻；每个人都携带较小的盾牌和较短的剑。

- instituted: 建立
- Philip: 菲利普
- military reforms: 军事改革
- transformed: 转变
- Macedonia: 马其顿
- major military power: 主要军事强国
- created: 创建
- new phalanx: 新方阵
- infantrymen: 步兵
- more lightly armed: 装备更轻
- Greek hoplites: 希腊重装步兵
- each carried: 每个人都携带
- smaller shield: 较小的盾牌
- shorter sword: 较短的剑
