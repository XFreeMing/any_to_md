## 观察

1. We must briefly examine prehistory and observe how human beings made the shift from hunting and gathering to agricultural communities and ultimately to cities and civilization. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 我们必须简要地审视史前史，并观察人类如何从狩猎和采集转向农业社区，最终转向城市和文明。

- observe: 观察
- briefly: 简要地
- examine: 审视
- prehistory: 史前史
- human beings: 人类
- made the shift: 转向
- hunting and gathering: 狩猎和采集
- agricultural communities: 农业社区
- ultimately: 最终
- cities: 城市
- civilization: 文明

2. Many of the conclusions presented in this book may already have occurred to you, for social psychological phenomena are all around you. We constantly observe people thinking about, influencing, and relating to one another. (Myers, 2012)

> 本书中提出的许多结论可能已经出现在你的脑海中，因为社会心理现象就在你身边。我们不断观察人们思考、影响和相互关联。

- observe: 观察
- conclusions: 结论
- presented: 提出
- occurred to: 出现在...脑海中
- social psychological phenomena: 社会心理现象
- all around you: 就在你身边
- constantly: 不断地
- thinking about: 思考
- influencing: 影响
- relating to: 关联

3. To a scientist, facts and theories are apples and oranges. Facts are agreed-upon statements about what we observe. Theories are ideas that summarize and explain facts. (Myers, 2012)

> 对科学家来说，事实和理论是不同的东西。事实是关于我们观察到的事物的公认陈述。理论是总结和解释事实的想法。

- observe: 观察
- scientist: 科学家
- facts: 事实
- theories: 理论
- apples and oranges: 不同的东西
- agreed-upon: 公认的
- statements: 陈述
- summarize: 总结
- explain: 解释

4. There is no single viewpoint from which to observe the mountains, trees, waters, and human habitations. (Oxford English Dictionary, 1989)

> 没有单一的视角可以观察山脉、树木、水域和人类居住地。

- observe: 观察
- single: 单一的
- viewpoint: 视角
- mountains: 山脉
- trees: 树木
- waters: 水域
- human habitations: 人类居住地
