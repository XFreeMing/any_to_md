1. The historical background has to do with the fact that as nations industrialize, their economies shift from agriculture to industry. (McConnell, Brue, & Flynn, 2014)

> 历史背景与这样一个事实有关：随着国家工业化，它们的经济从农业转向工业。

- industrialize: 工业化
- historical background: 历史背景
- has to do with: 与...有关
- fact: 事实
- as: 随着
- nations: 国家
- economies: 经济
- shift from: 从...转向
- agriculture: 农业
- industry: 工业

2. As a general rule, however, the later a country industrializes, the more extensive will be its state's economic role. In Japan and Germany, for instance, the state assumed a more active 'developmental' role from the outset. (Heywood, 2013)

> 然而，作为一般规则，一个国家工业化越晚，其国家的经济作用就越广泛。例如，在日本和德国，国家从一开始就承担了更积极的"发展"作用。

- industrialize: 工业化
- as a general rule: 作为一般规则
- however: 然而
- later: 越晚
- country: 国家
- more extensive: 越广泛
- state's economic role: 国家的经济作用
- Japan: 日本
- Germany: 德国
- for instance: 例如
- state: 国家
- assumed: 承担
- more active: 更积极的
- developmental role: 发展作用
- from the outset: 从一开始

3. The middle-income countries are primarily found in East and Southeast Asia, the oil-rich countries of the Middle East and North Africa, the Americas and the once-Communist republics that formerly made up the Soviet Union and its East European allies. Most of these countries began to industrialize relatively late in the twentieth century and are therefore not yet as industrially developed (or wealthy) as the high-income countries. (Giddens, 2009)

> 中等收入国家主要分布在东亚和东南亚、中东和北非的石油富国、美洲以及曾经组成苏联及其东欧盟友的前共产主义共和国。这些国家中的大多数在20世纪相对较晚才开始工业化，因此还没有像高收入国家那样工业发达（或富裕）。

- industrialize: 工业化
- middle-income countries: 中等收入国家
- primarily found in: 主要分布在

- oil-rich countries: 石油富国

- formerly made up: 曾经组成
- Soviet Union: 苏联
- East European allies: 东欧盟友
- most of these countries: 这些国家中的大多数
- began to: 开始
- relatively late: 相对较晚
- twentieth century: 20世纪
- therefore: 因此
- not yet: 还没有
- industrially developed: 工业发达
- wealthy: 富裕
- high-income countries: 高收入国家
