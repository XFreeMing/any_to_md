1. Recognizing the obvious military and industrial superiority of the West, the new leaders decided to modernize Japan by absorbing and adopting Western methods. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 认识到西方明显的军事和工业优势，新领导人决定通过吸收和采用西方方法来使日本现代化。

- modernize: 现代化
- recognizing: 认识到
- obvious: 明显的
- military superiority: 军事优势
- industrial superiority: 工业优势
- West: 西方
- new leaders: 新领导人
- decided to: 决定
- Japan: 日本
- absorbing: 吸收
- adopting: 采用
- Western methods: 西方方法

2. Over recent years, the use of technology in education has been utterly transformed. In most of the developed countries, education systems have been modernized and computerized. (Giddens, 2009)

> 近年来，技术在教育中的使用已经完全改变。在大多数发达国家，教育系统已经现代化和计算机化。

- modernize: 现代化
- over recent years: 近年来
- use of technology: 技术使用
- education: 教育
- utterly transformed: 完全改变
- most of: 大多数
- developed countries: 发达国家
- education systems: 教育系统
- computerized: 计算机化

3. Marx, Durkheim and Weber all theorize that a process of secularization was bound to occur as societies modernized and became more reliant on science and technology to control and explain the social world. (Giddens, 2009)

> 马克思、涂尔干和韦伯都理论化，随着社会现代化并越来越依赖科学和技术来控制和解释社会世界，世俗化过程必然会发生。

- modernize: 现代化
- Marx: 马克思
- Durkheim: 涂尔干
- Weber: 韦伯
- theorize: 理论化
- process of secularization: 世俗化过程
- bound to occur: 必然发生
- societies: 社会
- became more reliant on: 越来越依赖
- science and technology: 科学和技术
- control: 控制
- explain: 解释
- social world: 社会世界

- recent rush: 最近急于
- become: 成为
- world power: 世界强国
- Russia: 俄罗斯
- some respects: 某些方面
