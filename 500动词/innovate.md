1. Why work hard, or try to develop new products, if doing so will make a negligible impact on your income? Allowing at least some inequalities, on the other hand, will produce incentives for people to innovate and to work more productively. (Wolff, 2006)

> 如果这样做对你的收入影响微乎其微，为什么要努力工作或尝试开发新产品？另一方面，允许至少一些不平等，将为人们创新和更高效地工作产生激励。

- negligible impact: 微乎其微的影响
- on your income: 对你的收入
- allowing: 允许
- at least some inequalities: 至少一些不平等
- on the other hand: 另一方面
- will produce: 将产生
- incentives: 激励
- for people: 对人们
- to work more productively: 更高效地工作

- successful learning: 成功的学习

4.
