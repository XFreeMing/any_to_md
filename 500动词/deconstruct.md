1. More and more artists, such as Fred Wilson, began using familiar images in new contexts, revealing or deconstructing their deeper social, political, economic, and aesthetic meanings. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 越来越多的艺术家，如弗雷德·威尔逊，开始在新语境中使用熟悉的图像，揭示或解构它们更深层的社会、政治、经济和美学意义。

- deconstruct: 解构
- more and more artists: 越来越多的艺术家
- such as: 例如
- Fred Wilson: 弗雷德·威尔逊
- began using: 开始使用
- familiar images: 熟悉的图像
- new contexts: 新语境
- revealing: 揭示
- deeper social meanings: 更深层的社会意义
- political meanings: 政治意义
- economic meanings: 经济意义
- aesthetic meanings: 美学意义

2. As well as the recognition of difference between groups and individuals, postmodern feminists have stressed the importance of 'deconstruction'. In particular, they have sought to deconstruct male language and a masculine view of the world. (Giddens, 2009)

> 除了认识到群体和个人之间的差异，后现代女性主义者强调了"解构"的重要性。特别是，他们试图解构男性语言和男性世界观。

- deconstruct: 解构
- as well as: 除了
- recognition: 认识
- difference: 差异
- between: 在...之间
- groups: 群体
- individuals: 个人
- postmodern feminists: 后现代女性主义者
- stressed: 强调
- importance: 重要性
- deconstruction: 解构
- in particular: 特别是
- sought to: 试图
- male language: 男性语言
- masculine view: 男性世界观
- world: 世界

3. He is especially renowned for deconstructing museums, that is, reinstalling collections to reveal how museums have an agenda when they present art and how the interpretation of this art can change when it is put into a new context. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 他特别以解构博物馆而闻名，即重新安装收藏品以揭示博物馆在展示艺术时如何有议程，以及当艺术被置于新语境中时，对艺术的解释如何能够改变。

- deconstruct: 解构
- especially renowned for: 特别以...而闻名
- museums: 博物馆
- that is: 即
- reinstalling: 重新安装
- collections: 收藏品
- reveal: 揭示
- how museums have an agenda: 博物馆如何有议程
- present art: 展示艺术
- interpretation: 解释
- this art: 这种艺术
- can change: 能够改变
- put into: 被置于
- new context: 新语境

4. Language theorists suggested that one must "deconstruct" or "take apart" discourse in order to "unmask" its many meanings. (Giddens, 2009)

> 语言理论家建议，人们必须"解构"或"拆解"话语，以便"揭示"其许多意义。

- deconstruct: 解构
- language theorists: 语言理论家
- suggested: 建议
- one must: 人们必须
- take apart: 拆解
- discourse: 话语
- in order to: 以便
- unmask: 揭示
- many meanings: 许多意义
