1. Professionals in public service, who are high in cultural capital and low in economic capital, tend to pursue healthy, active lifestyles involving exercise, low alcohol consumption and participation in cultural and community activities. Managers and bureaucrats, by contrast, are typified by 'indistinctive' patterns of consumption, which involve average or low levels of exercise, little engagement with cultural activities, and a preference for traditional styles in home furnishings and fashion.

> 公共服务专业人士，他们拥有高文化资本和低经济资本，倾向于追求健康、积极的生活方式，包括锻炼、低酒精消费以及参与文化和社区活动。相比之下，管理者和官僚以"不显著"的消费模式为特征，这些模式涉及平均或低水平的锻炼、很少参与文化活动，以及对传统家居装饰和时尚风格的偏好。

- typify: 以...为特征，代表

- professionals: 专业人士

- public service: 公共服务

- cultural capital: 文化资本

- economic capital: 经济资本

- pursue: 追求

- healthy: 健康的

- active lifestyles: 积极的生活方式

- exercise: 锻炼

- alcohol consumption: 酒精消费

- participation: 参与

- cultural activities: 文化活动

- community activities: 社区活动

- managers: 管理者

- bureaucrats: 官僚

- indistinctive: 不显著的

- patterns of consumption: 消费模式

- engagement: 参与

- preference: 偏好

- traditional styles: 传统风格

- home furnishings: 家居装饰

- fashion: 时尚

  

2. The Lotus Mahal, like the entrance pavilion of Delhi's first mosque, exemplifies the stylistic crosscurrents that typify much of South Asian art and architecture of the second millennium. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 莲花宫，就像德里第一座清真寺的入口亭，体现了表征第二个千年南亚艺术和建筑大部分的风格交叉潮流。

- typify: 表征，代表

- Lotus Mahal: 莲花宫

- entrance pavilion: 入口亭

- Delhi's first mosque: 德里第一座清真寺

- exemplifies: 体现

- stylistic crosscurrents: 风格交叉潮流

- South Asian art: 南亚艺术

- architecture: 建筑

- second millennium: 第二个千年

  

3. Previously, we noted that seeing unresponsive bystanders makes us less likely to help. People reared by extremely punitive parents, as were many delinquents and chronic criminals, also show much less of the empathy and principled caring that typified altruists. (Myers, 2012)

> 之前，我们注意到看到无反应的旁观者使我们不太可能提供帮助。由极其惩罚性的父母抚养的人，就像许多少年犯和惯犯一样，也表现出远少于利他主义者所典型的同理心和有原则的关怀。

- typify: 以...为典型特征

- previously: 之前

- noted: 注意到

- unresponsive bystanders: 无反应的旁观者

- less likely: 不太可能

- help: 帮助

- reared: 抚养

- extremely punitive parents: 极其惩罚性的父母

- delinquents: 少年犯

- chronic criminals: 惯犯

- empathy: 同理心

- principled caring: 有原则的关怀

- altruists: 利他主义者

  

4. "That's unfair!" "What a ripoff!" "We deserve better!" Such comments typify conflicts bred by perceived injustice. (Giddens, 2009)

> "这不公平！" "这是敲诈！" "我们值得更好的！" 这样的评论代表了由感知到的不公正所引发的冲突。

- typify: 代表，典型地体现

- unfair: 不公平

- ripoff: 敲诈

- deserve: 值得

- comments: 评论

- conflicts: 冲突

- bred: 引发的

- perceived injustice: 感知到的不公正
