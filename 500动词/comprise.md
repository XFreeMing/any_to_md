1. The Alpine Mountain System comprises several mountain chains. Within it lie the Pyrenees, between Spain and France; the Alps in southeastern France, northern Italy, Switzerland, and western Austria, and the Apennine range in Italy. (Perry, 2010)

> 阿尔卑斯山系包括几条山脉。其中位于西班牙和法国之间的是比利牛斯山脉；位于法国东南部、意大利北部、瑞士和奥地利西部的是阿尔卑斯山脉；位于意大利的是亚平宁山脉。

- comprise: 包括
- Alpine Mountain System: 阿尔卑斯山系
- several: 几条
- mountain chains: 山脉
- within it lie: 其中位于
- Pyrenees: 比利牛斯山脉
- between: 在...之间
- Spain: 西班牙
- France: 法国
- Alps: 阿尔卑斯山脉
- southeastern: 东南部的
- northern: 北部的
- Italy: 意大利
- Switzerland: 瑞士
- western: 西部的
- Austria: 奥地利
- Apennine range: 亚平宁山脉

2. For one thousand animals are slaughtered daily for the king; these comprise horses, camels, oxen, asses, deer, and most of the small animals; many birds are also consumed, including Arabian ostriches---and the creature is large---geese, and cocks. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 因为每天为国王宰杀一千只动物；这些包括马、骆驼、牛、驴、鹿和大多数小动物；还消耗许多鸟类，包括阿拉伯鸵鸟——这种生物很大——鹅和公鸡。

- comprise: 包括
- one thousand: 一千
- animals: 动物
- slaughtered: 宰杀
- daily: 每天
- king: 国王
- horses: 马
- camels: 骆驼
- oxen: 牛
- asses: 驴
- deer: 鹿
- small animals: 小动物
- birds: 鸟类
- consumed: 消耗
- including: 包括
- Arabian ostriches: 阿拉伯鸵鸟
- creature: 生物
- large: 大的
- geese: 鹅
- cocks: 公鸡

3. This so-called Peasants' Crusade or Crusade of the Poor comprised a ragtag rabble that moved through the Balkans, terrorizing natives and looting for their food and supplies. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 这场所谓的农民十字军或穷人十字军包括一群乌合之众，他们穿过巴尔干半岛，恐吓当地人并抢劫食物和补给。

- comprise: 包括
- so-called: 所谓的
- Peasants' Crusade: 农民十字军
- Crusade of the Poor: 穷人十字军
- ragtag rabble: 乌合之众

- terrorizing: 恐吓
- natives: 当地人
- looting: 抢劫
- food: 食物
- supplies: 补给

4. The landholding class of nobles and knights comprised a military elite whose ability to function as warriors depended on having the leisure time to pursue the arts of war. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 拥有土地的贵族和骑士阶层构成了一个军事精英，他们作为战士发挥作用的能力取决于拥有追求战争艺术的闲暇时间。
