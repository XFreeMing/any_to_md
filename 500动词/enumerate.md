1. During the last few generations mankind has made an extraordinary advance in the natural sciences and in their technical application and has established his control over nature in a way never before imagined. The single steps of this advance are common knowledge and it is unnecessary to enumerate them. (Fiero, 2011f)

> 在过去的几代人中，人类在自然科学及其技术应用方面取得了非凡的进步，并以一种前所未有的方式确立了对自然的控制。这一进步的各个步骤是常识，没有必要一一列举。

- enumerate: 列举，一一列出

- generations: 代

- extraordinary advance: 非凡的进步

- natural sciences: 自然科学

- technical application: 技术应用

- established: 确立

- control over nature: 对自然的控制

- common knowledge: 常识

- unnecessary: 不必要的

  

2. The better-than-average effect may lead individuals to underestimate the probability of punishment in a criminal law context, overestimate the effectiveness of their precautions in tort law context, underestimate the probability of breaching in a contract context—indeed, the types of cases where better-than average-effect may distort the subjective evaluation of probabilities are too many to enumerate.

> 优于平均效应可能导致个人在刑法背景下低估惩罚的概率，在侵权法背景下高估其预防措施的有效性，在合同法背景下低估违约的概率——事实上，优于平均效应可能扭曲概率主观评估的案件类型太多，无法一一列举。

- enumerate: 列举

- better-than-average effect: 优于平均效应

- lead: 导致

- underestimate: 低估

- probability: 概率

- punishment: 惩罚

- criminal law context: 刑法背景

- overestimate: 高估

- effectiveness: 有效性

- precautions: 预防措施

- tort law context: 侵权法背景

- breaching: 违约

- contract context: 合同法背景

- distort: 扭曲

- subjective evaluation: 主观评估

  

3. New towns sprang up everywhere, achieving independence via charters that enumerated a town's privileges and immunities in return for a feudal lord's guarantee of protection. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 新城镇到处涌现，通过宪章获得独立，这些宪章列举了城镇的特权和豁免权，以换取封建领主的保护保证。

- enumerate: 列举

- sprang up: 涌现

- achieving: 获得

- independence: 独立

- via: 通过

- charters: 宪章

- privileges: 特权

- immunities: 豁免权

- in return for: 以换取

- feudal lord: 封建领主

- guarantee: 保证

- protection: 保护

  

4. Many other advantages might be enumerated. For instance, 200 the addition of some thousand carcasses in our exportation of barrelled beef, the propagation of swine's flesh, and improvement in the art of making good bacon, so much wanted among us by the great destruction of pigs, too frequent at our table, which are no way comparable in taste, or magnificence, to a well-grown, fat yearling child, which roasted whole will make a considerable figure at a Lord Mayor's feast, or any other public entertainment.

> 还可以列举许多其他优势。例如，在我们出口桶装牛肉时增加数千具尸体，传播猪肉，以及改进制作好培根的艺术，由于猪的大量毁灭，我们非常需要这些，在我们的餐桌上太频繁了，这些在味道或宏伟程度上都无法与一个发育良好、肥胖的一岁孩子相比，整只烤制将在市长宴会或任何其他公共娱乐活动中占据重要地位。

- enumerate: 列举

- advantages: 优势

- addition: 增加

- carcasses: 尸体

- exportation: 出口

- barrelled beef: 桶装牛肉

- propagation: 传播

- swine's flesh: 猪肉

- improvement: 改进

- art of making good bacon: 制作好培根的艺术

- destruction: 毁灭

- comparable: 可比较的

- magnificence: 宏伟

- well-grown: 发育良好的

- yearling child: 一岁孩子

- roasted whole: 整只烤制

- considerable figure: 重要地位

- Lord Mayor's feast: 市长宴会

- public entertainment: 公共娱乐
