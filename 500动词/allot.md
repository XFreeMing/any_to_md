1. Augustus inaugurated a new system for governing the provinces. Under the Republic, the senate had appointed the provincial governors. Now certain provinces were allotted to the princeps, who assigned deputies known as legates to govern them. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 奥古斯都开创了一个新的治理行省的系统。在共和国时期，元老院任命行省总督。现在某些行省被分配给元首，他指派被称为使节的副手来治理它们。

- provinces: 行省

- senate: 元老院
- appointed: 任命
- provincial governors: 行省总督
- certain provinces: 某些行省
- princeps: 元首
- assigned: 指派
- deputies: 副手
- known as: 被称为
- legates: 使节
- govern them: 治理它们
