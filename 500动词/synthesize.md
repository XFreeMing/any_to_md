1. It was Newton who synthesized them into a single scientific methodology by uniting Bacon's empiricism with Descartes' rationalism. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 是牛顿将它们综合成一种单一的科学方法论，通过将培根的经验主义与笛卡尔的理性主义结合起来。

- synthesize: 综合
- Newton: 牛顿
- single scientific methodology: 单一的科学方法论
- uniting: 结合
- Bacon's empiricism: 培根的经验主义
- Descartes' rationalism: 笛卡尔的理性主义

2. Searle's early work, which did a great deal to establish his reputation, was on speech acts. He attempted to synthesize ideas from many colleagues – including J. L. Austin, Ludwig Wittgenstein and G.C.J. Midgley – with his own thesis that such acts are constituted by the rules of language. ("John Searle," 2018)

> 塞尔早期的工作是关于言语行为的，这为建立他的声誉做了很多工作。他试图综合来自许多同事的想法——包括J. L. 奥斯汀、路德维希·维特根斯坦和G.C.J. 米奇利——与他自己的论点，即这些行为是由语言规则构成的。

- synthesize: 综合
- Searle's early work: 塞尔早期的工作
- did a great deal: 做了很多
- establish his reputation: 建立他的声誉
- speech acts: 言语行为
- attempted to: 试图
- ideas: 想法
- many colleagues: 许多同事
- including: 包括
- J. L. Austin: J. L. 奥斯汀
- Ludwig Wittgenstein: 路德维希·维特根斯坦
- G.C.J. Midgley: G.C.J. 米奇利
- own thesis: 自己的论点
- such acts: 这些行为
- constituted by: 由...构成
- rules of language: 语言规则

3. Piaget's concepts and ideas predated the cognitive revolution but inspired a wealth of research in the field of cognitive psychology and many of his principles have been blended with modern theory to synthesize the predominant views of today. ("Cognitive psychology," 2018)

> 皮亚杰的概念和思想早于认知革命，但启发了认知心理学领域的大量研究，他的许多原则已经与现代理论融合，综合成今天的主流观点。

- synthesize: 综合
- Piaget's concepts: 皮亚杰的概念
- ideas: 思想
- predated: 早于
- cognitive revolution: 认知革命
- inspired: 启发
- wealth of research: 大量研究
- field of cognitive psychology: 认知心理学领域
- principles: 原则
- blended with: 与...融合
- modern theory: 现代理论
- predominant views: 主流观点
- today: 今天

4. Dürer's painting technique owes much to the Flemish masters, but making copies of Italian works taught him many of the lessons of the Italian Renaissance. He was able to synthesize these traditions in his paintings and prints.

> 丢勒的绘画技巧很大程度上归功于佛兰芒大师，但制作意大利作品的副本教会了他意大利文艺复兴的许多经验。他能够在他的绘画和版画中综合这些传统。

- synthesize: 综合
- Dürer's painting technique: 丢勒的绘画技巧
- owes much to: 很大程度上归功于
- Flemish masters: 佛兰芒大师
- making copies: 制作副本
- Italian works: 意大利作品
- taught him: 教会了他
- lessons: 经验
- Italian Renaissance: 意大利文艺复兴
- able to: 能够
- traditions: 传统
- paintings: 绘画
- prints: 版画
