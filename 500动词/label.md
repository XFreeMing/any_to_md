1. The monuments he sponsored have a grandeur that justifies the claim that his era was a Golden Age, as some have labeled it. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 他赞助的纪念碑具有一种宏伟，证明了将他的时代称为"黄金时代"的说法是合理的，正如一些人所标记的那样。

- label: 标记，称为

- monuments: 纪念碑

- sponsored: 赞助

- grandeur: 宏伟

- justifies: 证明...合理

- claim: 说法

- era: 时代

- Golden Age: 黄金时代

- some: 一些人

  

2. Over the subsequent centuries, Leyster's paintings were either attributed to other artists, including Hals and Gerrit van Honthorst, or they were labeled artist unknown. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 在随后的几个世纪里，莱斯特的绘画要么被归因于其他艺术家，包括哈尔斯和格里特·范·洪特霍斯特，要么被标记为艺术家未知。

- label: 标记

- over: 在...期间

- subsequent centuries: 随后的几个世纪

- Leyster's paintings: 莱斯特的绘画

- either: 要么

- attributed to: 归因于

- other artists: 其他艺术家

- including: 包括

- Hals: 哈尔斯

- Gerrit van Honthorst: 格里特·范·洪特霍斯特

- artist unknown: 艺术家未知

  

3. The nobles, whose warlike attitudes were rationalized by labeling themselves the defenders of Christian society, continued to dominate the medieval world politically, economically, and socially. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 贵族们通过将自己标记为基督教社会的捍卫者来合理化他们的好战态度，继续在政治、经济和社会上统治中世纪世界。

- label: 标记

- nobles: 贵族

- warlike attitudes: 好战态度

- rationalized: 合理化

- defenders: 捍卫者

- Christian society: 基督教社会

- continued: 继续

- dominate: 统治

- medieval world: 中世纪世界

- politically: 政治上

- economically: 经济上

- socially: 社会上

  

4. In the history of Latin literature, the century and a half after Augustus is often labeled the ''silver age'' to indicate that the literary efforts of the period, though good, were not equal to the high standards of the Augustan golden age. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在拉丁文学史上，奥古斯都之后的一个半世纪通常被标记为"白银时代"，以表明这一时期的文学努力虽然很好，但没有达到奥古斯都黄金时代的高标准。

- label: 标记

- history: 历史

- Latin literature: 拉丁文学

- century and a half: 一个半世纪

- after: 之后

- Augustus: 奥古斯都

- often: 通常

- silver age: 白银时代

- indicate: 表明

- literary efforts: 文学努力

- period: 时期

- though: 虽然

- good: 好的

- not equal to: 没有达到

- high standards: 高标准

- Augustan golden age: 奥古斯都黄金时代
