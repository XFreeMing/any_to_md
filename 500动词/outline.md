3. In Emile (1762), his treatise on education, Rousseau advanced the hypothesis—unheard of in his time—that the education of a child begins at birth. He divided childhood development into five stages over a twenty-five-year span and outlined the type of rearing desirable for each stage. (Fiero, 2011d)

> 在《爱弥儿》（1762）中，他关于教育的论文，卢梭提出了一个在他那个时代闻所未闻的假设——孩子的教育从出生开始。他将童年发展分为五个阶段，跨越25年，并概述了每个阶段理想的养育类型。

4. In the Imagist Manifesto (1913) and in various interviews, Pound outlined the cardinal points of the Imagist doctrine: poets should use "absolutely no word that does not contribute to the presentation"; they should employ free verse rhythms "in sequence of the musical phrase." (Fiero, 2011f)
