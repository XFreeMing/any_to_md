1. According to Mohammadi, resistance against the incursion of outside media forms has ranged from muted criticism to the outright banning of Western satellites. (Myers, 2012)

> 根据穆罕默迪的说法，对外部媒体形式入侵的抵制范围从温和的批评到彻底禁止西方卫星。

- range: 范围，在...范围内变化

- resistance: 抵制

- incursion: 入侵

- outside media forms: 外部媒体形式

- muted criticism: 温和的批评

- outright banning: 彻底禁止

- Western satellites: 西方卫星

  

2. Medici acquisitions ranged from mythological to biblical to contemporary historical subject matter and included both paintings and sculptures. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 美第奇家族的收藏范围从神话到圣经再到当代历史题材，包括绘画和雕塑。

- range: 范围，涵盖

- acquisitions: 收藏

- mythological: 神话的

- biblical: 圣经的

- contemporary: 当代的

- historical subject matter: 历史题材

- paintings: 绘画

- sculptures: 雕塑

  

3. Like Marx, Max Weber (1864 - 1920) cannot simply be labelled a sociologist; his interests and concerns ranged across many areas. (Giddens, 2009)

> 像马克思一样，马克斯·韦伯（1864-1920）不能简单地被贴上社会学家的标签；他的兴趣和关注点涉及许多领域。

- range: 涉及，跨越

- labelled: 被贴上标签

- sociologist: 社会学家

- interests: 兴趣

- concerns: 关注点

- areas: 领域

  

4. Carved of soft gypsum and inlaid with shell and black limestone, the statuettes ranged in size from well under a foot to about 30 inches tall. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 这些小雕像由软石膏雕刻而成，镶嵌着贝壳和黑色石灰石，尺寸从远小于一英尺到约30英寸高不等。

- range: 在...范围内变化

- carved: 雕刻的

- soft gypsum: 软石膏

- inlaid: 镶嵌的

- shell: 贝壳

- black limestone: 黑色石灰石

- statuettes: 小雕像

- size: 尺寸
