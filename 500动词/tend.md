## 倾向于

1. Hence the collectively rational position is unstable, and individuals will tend to defect, even if they know the consequences of everyone acting that way. (Wolff, 2006)

> 因此，集体理性的立场是不稳定的，个人往往会背叛，即使他们知道每个人都这样做的后果。

- tend: 倾向于
- hence: 因此
- collectively rational position: 集体理性的立场
- unstable: 不稳定的
- individuals: 个人
- defect: 背叛
- even if: 即使
- know: 知道
- consequences: 后果
- acting that way: 这样做

2. For this map, it is important to identify the symbols for universities and schools and to see where they appear on the map. Because education does not tend to move as a wave, as the plague did, each symbol represents a place where learning flourished more than it did in places without a symbol of some kind. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 对于这张地图，重要的是识别大学和学校的符号，并查看它们在地图上的位置。因为教育不像瘟疫那样倾向于像波浪一样移动，每个符号代表一个学习比没有某种符号的地方更繁荣的地方。

- tend: 倾向于
- map: 地图
- important: 重要的
- identify: 识别
- symbols: 符号
- universities: 大学
- schools: 学校
- appear: 出现
- education: 教育
- move as a wave: 像波浪一样移动
- plague: 瘟疫
- each symbol: 每个符号
- represents: 代表
- place: 地方
- learning: 学习
- flourished: 繁荣
- without: 没有
- some kind: 某种

3. The skeptical viewpoint tends to underplay this experiential aspect of the process. (Giddens, 2009)

> 怀疑的观点倾向于低估这个过程的经验方面。

- tend: 倾向于
- skeptical viewpoint: 怀疑的观点
- underplay: 低估
- experiential aspect: 经验方面
- process: 过程

4. When someone outperforms them, people tend to think of the other as a genius (Lassiter & Munhall, 2001). (Myers, 2012)

> 当有人超越他们时，人们倾向于认为对方是天才（拉西特和芒霍尔，2001）。

- tend: 倾向于
- when: 当...时
- someone: 某人
- outperforms: 超越
- people: 人们
- think of: 认为
- other: 对方
- genius: 天才
