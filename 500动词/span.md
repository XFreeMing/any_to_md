1. The emergence of a unified global economy, with business whose factories, management structures and markets often span continents and countries. (Giddens, 2009)

> 统一全球经济的出现，其企业的工厂、管理结构和市场往往跨越大陆和国家。

- span: 跨越

- emergence: 出现

- unified: 统一的

- global economy: 全球经济

- factories: 工厂

- management structures: 管理结构

- continents: 大陆

  

2. Durkheim's writings spanned a broad spectrum of topics. Three of the main themes he addressed were the importance of sociology as an empirical science, the rise of the individual and the formation of a new social order, and the sources and character of moral authority in society. (Giddens, 2009)

> 涂尔干的著作涵盖了广泛的主题。他论述的三个主要主题是：社会学作为经验科学的重要性、个人的崛起和新社会秩序的形成，以及社会中道德权威的来源和特征。

- span: 涵盖

- writings: 著作

- broad spectrum: 广泛的范围

- topics: 主题

- addressed: 论述

- empirical science: 经验科学

- formation: 形成

- social order: 社会秩序

- moral authority: 道德权威

  

3. Jasper Johns (b. 1930), an artist whose career has spanned more than half a century, shared Warhol's interest in commonplace objects. (Fiero, 2011f)

> 贾斯珀·约翰斯（生于1930年）是一位职业生涯跨越了半个多世纪的艺术家，他与沃霍尔一样对普通物品感兴趣。

- span: 跨越

- career: 职业生涯

- shared: 分享，共同拥有

- commonplace: 普通的

- objects: 物品

  

4. As a result, the cultural map of the world changes: networks of peoples span national borders and even continents, providing cultural connections between their birthplaces and their adoptive countries (Appadurai 1986). (Giddens, 2009)

> 结果，世界的文化地图发生了变化：人们的网络跨越了国界甚至大陆，在他们的出生地和收养国之间提供了文化联系。

- span: 跨越

- cultural map: 文化地图

- networks: 网络

- national borders: 国界

- continents: 大陆

- providing: 提供

- cultural connections: 文化联系

- birthplaces: 出生地

- adoptive countries: 收养国
