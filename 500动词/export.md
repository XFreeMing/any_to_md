1. Ottoman ceramics and silks were exported in large quantities to Russia and Europe, where along with the much-prized carpets from Asia Minor they were quickly absorbed into European material culture. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 奥斯曼陶瓷和丝绸大量出口到俄罗斯和欧洲，在那里，连同来自小亚细亚的珍贵地毯，它们很快被吸收到欧洲物质文化中。

- export: 出口
- Ottoman ceramics: 奥斯曼陶瓷
- silks: 丝绸
- large quantities: 大量
- Russia: 俄罗斯
- Europe: 欧洲
- along with: 连同
- much-prized: 珍贵的
- carpets: 地毯
- Asia Minor: 小亚细亚
- quickly absorbed: 很快被吸收
- European material culture: 欧洲物质文化

2. Trends in increasing consumption therefore encourage developing countries to export their natural commodities——a process which results in both environmental destruction and a loss of biodiversity. (Giddens, 2009)

> 因此，消费增长的趋势鼓励发展中国家出口它们的天然商品——这一过程导致环境破坏和生物多样性的丧失。

- export: 出口
- trends: 趋势
- increasing consumption: 消费增长
- therefore: 因此
- encourage: 鼓励
- developing countries: 发展中国家
- natural commodities: 天然商品
- process: 过程
- results in: 导致
- both: 既
- environmental destruction: 环境破坏
- loss: 丧失
- biodiversity: 生物多样性

3. In any case, Europeans had begun to change the face of the world and increasingly saw their culture, with its religion, languages, and technology, as a coherent force to be exported to all corners of the earth. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 无论如何，欧洲人已经开始改变世界的面貌，并越来越多地将他们的文化，包括其宗教、语言和技术，视为一种可以输出到地球各个角落的连贯力量。

- export: 输出
- in any case: 无论如何
- Europeans: 欧洲人
- begun to: 开始
- change: 改变
- face of the world: 世界的面貌
- increasingly: 越来越多地
- saw: 视为
- culture: 文化
- religion: 宗教
- languages: 语言
- technology: 技术
- coherent force: 连贯力量
- all corners of the earth: 地球各个角落

4. The radicals want to carry on with and deepen the Islamic revolution; they also believe that the revolution should be actively exported to other Islamic countries.

> 激进分子想要继续并深化伊斯兰革命；他们也相信革命应该积极输出到其他伊斯兰国家。

- export: 输出
- radicals: 激进分子
- want to: 想要
- carry on with: 继续
- deepen: 深化
- Islamic revolution: 伊斯兰革命
- also: 也
- believe: 相信
- revolution: 革命
- actively: 积极地
- other Islamic countries: 其他伊斯兰国家
