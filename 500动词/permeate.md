1. According to the critics, including most sociologists influenced by symbolic interactionism, as human beings we have reasons for what we do, and we inhabit a social world permeated by cultural meanings. (Giddens, 2009)

> 根据批评者的说法，包括大多数受符号互动论影响的社会学家，作为人类，我们对自己所做的事情有理由，我们生活在一个充满文化意义的社会世界中。

- permeate: 充满
- according to: 根据
- critics: 批评者
- including: 包括
- most sociologists: 大多数社会学家
- influenced by: 受...影响
- symbolic interactionism: 符号互动论
- human beings: 人类
- reasons: 理由
- inhabit: 生活
- social world: 社会世界
- cultural meanings: 文化意义

2. Religion permeated every aspect of Egyptian life. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 宗教渗透到埃及生活的每个方面。

- permeate: 渗透
- religion: 宗教
- every aspect: 每个方面
- Egyptian life: 埃及生活

3. Television permeates the daily life of millions of people and portrays considerable violence. (Myers, 2012)

> 电视渗透到数百万人的日常生活中，并描绘了大量的暴力。

- permeate: 渗透
- television: 电视
- daily life: 日常生活
- millions of people: 数百万人
- portrays: 描绘
- considerable: 大量的
- violence: 暴力

4. An enormous amount of artistic talent was poured into the construction of churches, church ceremonies, and church decoration. Spiritual principles deeply permeated Byzantine art.

> 大量的艺术才能被投入到教堂的建造、教堂仪式和教堂装饰中。精神原则深深渗透到拜占庭艺术中。

- permeate: 渗透
- enormous amount: 大量的
- artistic talent: 艺术才能
- poured into: 投入到
- construction: 建造
- churches: 教堂
- church ceremonies: 教堂仪式
- church decoration: 教堂装饰
- spiritual principles: 精神原则
- deeply: 深深地
- Byzantine art: 拜占庭艺术
