1. Historians rely primarily on documents to create their pictures of the past, but no written records exist for the prehistory of humankind. In their absence, the story of early humanity depends on archaeological and, more recently, biological information, which anthropologists and archaeologists use to formulate theories about our early past. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 历史学家主要依靠文献来创建他们对过去的图景，但人类史前时期没有书面记录。在缺乏这些记录的情况下，早期人类的故事依赖于考古学信息，以及最近的生物学信息，人类学家和考古学家用这些信息来构建关于我们早期过去的理论。

- written records: 书面记录
- exist for: 存在于
- prehistory of humankind: 人类史前时期
- in their absence: 在缺乏这些记录的情况下
- story of early humanity: 早期人类的故事
- depends on: 依赖于
- archaeological: 考古学的
- more recently: 最近的
- biological information: 生物学信息
- anthropologists: 人类学家
- archaeologists: 考古学家
- use to: 用...来
- theories: 理论
- about our early past: 关于我们早期过去

2. Poststructuralism, or deconstruction, which was formulated by Jacques Derrida (1930--2004), believes that culture is created and can therefore be analyzed in a variety of ways, according to the manner in which people create their own meaning. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 后结构主义或解构主义，由雅克·德里达（1930-2004）构建，认为文化是被创造的，因此可以根据人们创造自己意义的方式以多种方式进行分析。

- poststructuralism: 后结构主义
