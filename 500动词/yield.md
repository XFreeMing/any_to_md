1. In the last section I suggested that a utilitarian political philosophy would probably yield a free market with a welfare state. (Wolff, 2006)

> 在上一节中，我建议功利主义政治哲学可能会产生一个带有福利国家的自由市场。

- yield: 产生
- last section: 上一节
- suggested: 建议
- utilitarian political philosophy: 功利主义政治哲学
- probably: 可能
- free market: 自由市场
- welfare state: 福利国家

2. A pair of Fourth Dynasty sculptures from a mastaba tomb at Meidum are carved from limestone, which is softer than diorite and does not yield such fine surface detail. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 一对来自迈杜姆马斯塔巴墓的第四王朝雕塑是用石灰石雕刻的，石灰石比闪长岩软，不能产生如此精细的表面细节。


- yield: 产生
- a pair of: 一对
- Fourth Dynasty: 第四王朝
- sculptures: 雕塑
- mastaba tomb: 马斯塔巴墓
- Meidum: 梅杜姆
- carved from: 用...雕刻
- limestone: 石灰石
- softer than: 比...软
- diorite: 闪长岩
- does not: 不能
- fine surface detail: 精细的表面细节

3. Florence's woolen industry, one of the giants, produced 70,000 to 80,000 pieces of cloth in 1338; in 1378, it was yielding only 24,000 pieces. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 佛罗伦萨的毛纺工业，作为巨头之一，在 1338 年生产了 7 万到 8 万件布料；在 1378 年，它只产出 2.4 万件。

- yield: 产出
- Florence's woolen industry: 佛罗伦萨的毛纺工业
- one of the giants: 巨头之一
- produced: 生产
- pieces of cloth: 件布料
- was yielding: 正在产出
- only: 只

4. Although reason might yield a true understanding of nature, it could in no way prove God's existence. (Fiero, 2011)

> 虽然理性可能产生对自然的真正理解，但它绝不可能证明上帝的存在。

- yield: 产生
- although: 虽然
- reason: 理性
- might: 可能
- true understanding: 真正理解
- nature: 自然
- could in no way: 绝不可能
- prove: 证明
- God's existence: 上帝的存在
