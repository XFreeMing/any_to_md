1. The steps lead to a deep porch with rows of columns, and to the cella beyond, which was often subdivided into three compartments. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 台阶通向一个有成排柱子的深门廊，以及后面的内殿，内殿经常被细分为三个隔间。

- subdivide: 细分
- steps: 台阶
- lead to: 通向
- deep porch: 深门廊
- rows of columns: 成排柱子
- cella: 内殿
- beyond: 后面
- often: 经常
- three compartments: 三个隔间

2. Scholars divide the Aegean Bronze Age into three phases: Early, Middle, and Late, each of which is further subdivided into three phases, I, II, and III. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 学者们将爱琴青铜时代分为三个阶段：早期、中期和晚期，每个阶段又进一步细分为三个阶段，I、II和III。

- subdivide: 细分
- scholars: 学者们
- divide: 分为
- Aegean Bronze Age: 爱琴青铜时代
- three phases: 三个阶段
- Early: 早期
- Middle: 中期
- Late: 晚期
- each of which: 每个阶段
- further: 进一步
- three phases: 三个阶段
- I, II, and III: I、II和III

3. The house was subdivided into apartments so as to gain more benefits when rented.

> 这所房子被细分为公寓，以便在出租时获得更多收益。

- subdivide: 细分
- house: 房子
- apartments: 公寓
- so as to: 以便
- gain: 获得
- more benefits: 更多收益
- when rented: 在出租时

4. To reorganize local government, Catherine divided Russia into fifty provinces, each of which was in turn subdivided into districts ruled by officials chosen by the nobles. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 为了重组地方政府，叶卡捷琳娜将俄罗斯分为五十个省，每个省又依次细分为由贵族选择的官员统治的区。

- subdivide: 细分
- reorganize: 重组
- local government: 地方政府
- Catherine: 叶卡捷琳娜
- divided: 分为
- Russia: 俄罗斯
- fifty provinces: 五十个省
- each of which: 每个省
- in turn: 依次
- districts: 区
- ruled by: 由...统治
- officials: 官员
- chosen by: 由...选择
- nobles: 贵族
