1. The mind, according to Kant, is not a passive recipient of information (Locke's "blank slate"); rather, it participates in the process of knowing the world. Concepts such as time, space, and causality are innate conditions that organize experience. (Fiero, 2011d)

> 根据康德的观点，心灵不是信息的被动接受者（洛克的"白板"）；相反，它参与认识世界的过程。时间、空间和因果性等概念是组织经验的先天条件。

- organize: 组织

- mind: 心灵

- according to: 根据

- Kant: 康德

- passive recipient: 被动接受者

- information: 信息

- blank slate: 白板

- rather: 相反

- participates: 参与

- process: 过程

- knowing the world: 认识世界

- concepts: 概念

- time: 时间

- space: 空间

- causality: 因果性

- innate conditions: 先天条件

- experience: 经验

  

2. Rococo composers abandoned the intricate counterpoint and dense textures of the Baroque in favor of light and graceful melodies organized into short, distinct phrases. (Fiero, 2011d)

> 洛可可作曲家放弃了巴洛克复杂的对位法和密集的织体，转而采用组织成短小、清晰乐句的轻快优雅的旋律。

- organize: 组织

- Rococo composers: 洛可可作曲家

- abandoned: 放弃

- intricate counterpoint: 复杂的对位法

- dense textures: 密集的织体

- Baroque: 巴洛克

- in favor of: 转而采用

- light: 轻快的

- graceful melodies: 优雅的旋律

- short: 短小的

- distinct phrases: 清晰的乐句

  

3. Books of hours, in which prayers were organized into cycles according to the hours of the day, appealed especially to women. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 祈祷书，其中祈祷按照一天中的时间组织成循环，特别吸引女性。

- organize: 组织

- Books of hours: 祈祷书

- prayers: 祈祷

- cycles: 循环

- according to: 按照

- hours of the day: 一天中的时间

- appealed: 吸引

- especially: 特别

- women: 女性

  

4. Mining communities initially organized to protest against the policy, but, when this eventually failed, many individual miners retrained to find work in other industries. (Giddens, 2009)

> 采矿社区最初组织起来抗议这项政策，但当这最终失败时，许多个别矿工重新培训以在其他行业找工作。

- organize: 组织

- mining communities: 采矿社区

- initially: 最初

- protest against: 抗议

- policy: 政策

- eventually: 最终

- failed: 失败

- individual miners: 个别矿工

- retrained: 重新培训

- find work: 找工作

- other industries: 其他行业
