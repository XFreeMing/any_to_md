1. To the woman, since he had made her body less capable of such endurance, I take it that God has assigned the indoor tasks. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 对女人来说，既然他使她的身体不太能够承受这样的耐力，我认为上帝已经分配了室内任务。

- assign: 分配
- woman: 女人
- since: 既然
- made her body: 使她的身体
- less capable of: 不太能够
- such endurance: 这样的耐力
- I take it that: 我认为
- God: 上帝
- indoor tasks: 室内任务

2. Germany is a young and growing Empire. She has a world-wide commerce, which is rapidly expanding, and to which the legitimate ambition of patriotic Germans refuses to assign any bounds. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 德国是一个年轻且不断增长的帝国。她拥有世界范围的商业，正在迅速扩张，爱国的德国人的合法雄心拒绝为其设定任何界限。

- assign: 设定
- Germany: 德国
- young and growing Empire: 年轻且不断增长的帝国
- world-wide commerce: 世界范围的商业
- rapidly expanding: 迅速扩张
- legitimate ambition: 合法雄心
- patriotic Germans: 爱国的德国人
- refuses to: 拒绝
- any bounds: 任何界限

3. Initially, scholars assigned relative dates to cave paintings by using stylistic analysis, dating them according to their degree of naturalism, that is, how closely the image resembled the subject in nature. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 最初，学者们通过使用风格分析为洞穴绘画分配相对日期，根据它们的自然主义程度来确定日期，也就是说，图像与自然界中的主题有多相似。

- assign: 分配
- initially: 最初
- scholars: 学者们
- relative dates: 相对日期
- cave paintings: 洞穴绘画
- using: 使用
- stylistic analysis: 风格分析
- dating them: 确定它们的日期
- according to: 根据
- degree of naturalism: 自然主义程度
- that is: 也就是说
- how closely: 有多相似
- image: 图像
- resembled: 相似
- subject: 主题
- in nature: 在自然界中

4. They assign causes for lightning, winds, eclipses, and other 80 inexplicable things, never hesitating a whit, as if they were privy to the secrets of nature, artificer of things, or as if they visited us fresh from the council of the gods.

> 他们为闪电、风、日食和其他80种无法解释的事物分配原因，从不犹豫一丝一毫，好像他们知道自然的秘密、事物的创造者，或者好像他们刚从众神的议会访问我们。

- assign: 分配
- they: 他们
- causes: 原因
- lightning: 闪电
- winds: 风
- eclipses: 日食
- other 80 inexplicable things: 其他80种无法解释的事物
- never hesitating: 从不犹豫
- a whit: 一丝一毫
- as if: 好像
- privy to: 知道
- secrets of nature: 自然的秘密
- artificer of things: 事物的创造者
- visited us: 访问我们
- fresh from: 刚从
- council of the gods: 众神的议会
