1. Studies of jaywalking behavior, conducted with the unwitting aid of nearly 24,000 pedestrians, reveal that the baseline jaywalking rate of 25 percent decreases to 17 percent in the presence of a nonjaywalking confederate and increases to 44 percent in the presence of another jaywalker (Mullen & others, 1990). (Myers, 2012)

> 对乱穿马路行为的研究，在近24,000名行人的无意帮助下进行，揭示了基线乱穿马路率为25%，在非乱穿马路同伙的存在下降低到17%，在另一个乱穿马路者的存在下增加到44%（马伦等人，1990）。

- conduct: 进行
- studies: 研究
- jaywalking behavior: 乱穿马路行为
- unwitting aid: 无意帮助
- nearly: 近
- pedestrians: 行人
- reveal: 揭示
- baseline: 基线
- jaywalking rate: 乱穿马路率
- 25 percent: 25%
- decreases to: 降低到
- 17 percent: 17%
- in the presence of: 在...存在下
- nonjaywalking confederate: 非乱穿马路同伙
- increases to: 增加到
- 44 percent: 44%
- another jaywalker: 另一个乱穿马路者
