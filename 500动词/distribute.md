3. When people have become accustomed to observe the fundamental rules of social life, and their labor is so productive that they voluntarily work according to their ability. . .. There will then be no need for any exact calculation by society of the quantity of products to be distributed to each of its members; each will take freely according to his needs. (Fiero, 2011f)
