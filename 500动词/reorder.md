1. Even most rural areas do not escape the influence of human intervention and modern technology, for human activity has reshaped and reordered the world of nature. (Giddens, 2009)

> 即使大多数农村地区也无法逃脱人类干预和现代技术的影响，因为人类活动已经重塑和重新排序了自然世界。

- reorder: 重新排序
- even: 即使
- most rural areas: 大多数农村地区
- do not escape: 无法逃脱
- influence: 影响
- human intervention: 人类干预
- modern technology: 现代技术
- human activity: 人类活动
- reshaped: 重塑
- world of nature: 自然世界

2. Historians often define the eighteenth century as the years from 1715 to 1789. This period has often been portrayed as the final phase of Europe's old order, swept away by the violent upheaval and reordering of society associated with the French Revolution. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 历史学家经常将18世纪定义为从1715年到1789年的年份。这个时期经常被描绘为欧洲旧秩序的最后阶段，被与法国革命相关的暴力动荡和社会的重新排序所扫除。

- reorder: 重新排序
- historians: 历史学家
- often define: 经常定义
- eighteenth century: 18世纪
- years from 1715 to 1789: 从1715年到1789年的年份
- period: 时期
- portrayed as: 被描绘为
- final phase: 最后阶段
- Europe's old order: 欧洲旧秩序
- swept away: 被扫除
- violent upheaval: 暴力动荡
- reordering of society: 社会的重新排序
- associated with: 与...相关
- French Revolution: 法国革命

3. Whole industries and markets are being radically reordered by the electronic revolution. (Williamson, 2013)

> 整个行业和市场正在被电子革命彻底重新排序。

- reorder: 重新排序
- whole industries: 整个行业
- markets: 市场
- being radically: 正在彻底地
- electronic revolution: 电子革命

4. The whole system needs to be reordered. (Giddens, 2009)

> 整个系统需要被重新排序。

- reorder: 重新排序
- whole system: 整个系统
- needs to be: 需要被
