1. The Persian kings lived in luxury as a result of their conquests and ability to extract taxes from their conquered subjects. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 波斯国王过着奢侈的生活，这是他们征服和从被征服的臣民那里征收税收的能力的结果。

- extract: 征收
- Persian kings: 波斯国王
- lived in luxury: 过着奢侈的生活
- result of: ...的结果
- conquests: 征服
- ability: 能力
- taxes: 税收
- conquered subjects: 被征服的臣民

2. Under colonial rule, for example, the petroleum, copper, iron and food products required by industrial economies are extracted from low-income countries by businesses based in high income countries. (Giddens, 2009)

> 例如，在殖民统治下，工业经济所需的石油、铜、铁和食品由基于高收入国家的企业从低收入国家提取。

- extract: 提取
- under colonial rule: 在殖民统治下
- for example: 例如
- petroleum: 石油
- copper: 铜
- iron: 铁
- food products: 食品
- required by: 所需的
- industrial economies: 工业经济
- low-income countries: 低收入国家
- businesses: 企业
- based in: 基于
- high income countries: 高收入国家

3. The capitalist revolution broke the bounds of traditional feudal production systems, demanding a new discipline and long hours from workers so that capitalists could extract a profit from using their labour power. (Giddens, 2009)

> 资本主义革命打破了传统封建生产制度的界限，要求工人有新的纪律和长时间工作，以便资本家可以从使用他们的劳动力中提取利润。

- extract: 提取
- capitalist revolution: 资本主义革命
- broke the bounds: 打破界限
- traditional feudal production systems: 传统封建生产制度
- demanding: 要求
- new discipline: 新纪律
- long hours: 长时间
- workers: 工人
- capitalists: 资本家
- profit: 利润
- using: 使用
- labour power: 劳动力

4. The more you know about the author, the more meaningful and reliable information you can extract from the document.

> 你对作者了解得越多，你就能从文件中提取越有意义和可靠的信息。

- extract: 提取
- more you know: 你了解得越多
- about the author: 关于作者
- more meaningful: 越有意义
- reliable information: 可靠的信息
- document: 文件
