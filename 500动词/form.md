1. As we have seen, early human beings formed small groups that developed a simple culture that enabled them to survive. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 正如我们所看到的，早期人类形成了小群体，这些群体发展出一种简单的文化，使他们能够生存。

- formed: 形成
- as we have seen: 正如我们所看到的
- early human beings: 早期人类
- small groups: 小群体
- developed: 发展
- simple culture: 简单的文化
- enabled them: 使他们
- to survive: 生存

2. The Persians were an Indo-European-speaking people related to the Medes. Both peoples probably formed part of the great waves of Indo-European migrations into the Mediterranean, the Near East, and India. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 波斯人是与米底人相关的印欧语系民族。两个民族可能都形成了进入地中海、近东和印度的印欧语系大迁徙浪潮的一部分。

- formed: 形成
- Persians: 波斯人
- Indo-European-speaking people: 印欧语系民族
- related to: 与...相关
- Medes: 米底人
- both peoples: 两个民族
- probably: 可能
- part of: ...的一部分
- great waves: 大浪潮
- Indo-European migrations: 印欧语系迁徙
- into: 进入
- Mediterranean: 地中海
- Near East: 近东
- India: 印度

3. Primarily nomadic, both Medes and Persians were organized in clans. Leaders of both peoples were petty kings assisted by a group of warriors who formed a class of nobles. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 主要是游牧的，米底人和波斯人都以氏族组织。两个民族的领袖都是小国王，由一群形成贵族阶级的战士协助。

- formed: 形成
- primarily nomadic: 主要是游牧的
- both Medes and Persians: 米底人和波斯人
- organized in clans: 以氏族组织
- leaders: 领袖
- both peoples: 两个民族
- petty kings: 小国王
- assisted by: 由...协助
- group of warriors: 一群战士
- class of nobles: 贵族阶级

4. In preparation for the attack, some of the Greek states formed a defensive league under Spartan leadership, while the Athenians pursued a new military policy by developing a navy. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 为了准备攻击，一些希腊城邦在斯巴达的领导下形成了一个防御联盟，而雅典人通过发展海军追求新的军事政策。

- formed: 形成
- in preparation for: 为了准备
- the attack: 攻击
- some of the Greek states: 一些希腊城邦
- defensive league: 防御联盟
- under Spartan leadership: 在斯巴达的领导下
- Athenians: 雅典人
- pursued: 追求
- new military policy: 新的军事政策
- by developing: 通过发展
- navy: 海军
