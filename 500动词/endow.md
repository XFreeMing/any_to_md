1. We should not fear the gods we ourselves have created, and we should cease endowing them with values we ourselves can realize. (Giddens, 2009)

> 我们不应该害怕我们自己创造的神，我们应该停止赋予它们我们自己能够实现的价值。

- endow: 赋予
- should not fear: 不应该害怕
- gods: 神
- ourselves have created: 我们自己创造的
- cease: 停止
- values: 价值
- ourselves can realize: 我们自己能够实现的

2. Everyone, I know, will admit that it would be most laudable for a Prince to be endowed with all of the above qualities that are reckoned good. (Fiero, 2011c)

> 我知道，每个人都会承认，如果一位王子被赋予所有上述被认为是好的品质，那将是最值得称赞的。

- endow: 赋予
- everyone: 每个人
- admit: 承认
- would be: 将是
- most laudable: 最值得称赞的
- Prince: 王子
- all of the above qualities: 所有上述品质
- reckoned good: 被认为是好的

3. Although biology (for example, in the form of male and female hormones) plays an important role in gender differences, social roles are also a major influence. What's agreed is that nature endows us with a remarkable capacity to adapt to differing contexts. (Myers, 2012)

> 虽然生物学（例如，以男性和女性激素的形式）在性别差异中发挥重要作用，但社会角色也是一个主要影响因素。大家一致认为，自然赋予我们适应不同环境的非凡能力。

- endow: 赋予
- although: 虽然
- biology: 生物学
- for example: 例如
- form: 形式
- male and female hormones: 男性和女性激素
- plays an important role: 发挥重要作用
- gender differences: 性别差异
- social roles: 社会角色
- major influence: 主要影响因素
- what's agreed: 大家一致认为
- nature: 自然
- remarkable capacity: 非凡能力
- adapt to: 适应
- differing contexts: 不同环境

4. If women were also endowed with reason, why should men alone determine the standards and ground rules, she asked pungently.

> 如果女性也被赋予理性，为什么应该只有男性决定标准和基本规则，她尖锐地问道。

- endow: 赋予
- if: 如果
- women: 女性
- also: 也
- reason: 理性
- why: 为什么
- should: 应该
- men alone: 只有男性
- determine: 决定
- standards: 标准
- ground rules: 基本规则
- asked: 问道
- pungently: 尖锐地
