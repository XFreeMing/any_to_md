3. Because aggression is a complex behavior, no one spot in the brain controls it. But researchers have found brain neural systems in both animals and humans that facilitate aggression. When the scientists activate these brain areas, hostility increases; when they deactivate them, hostility decreases. (Myers, 2012)

> 因为攻击性是一种复杂的行为，大脑中没有单一位置控制它。但研究人员在动物和人类中都发现了促进攻击性的大脑神经系统。当科学家激活这些大脑区域时，敌意增加；当他们停用它们时，敌意减少。

- activate: 激活
- because: 因为
- aggression: 攻击性
- complex behavior: 复杂的行为
- no one spot: 没有单一位置
- in the brain: 在大脑中
- controls it: 控制它
- researchers: 研究人员
- found: 发现
- brain neural systems: 大脑神经系统
- both animals and humans: 动物和人类
- facilitate: 促进
- scientists: 科学家
- these brain areas: 这些大脑区域
- hostility increases: 敌意增加
- deactivate: 停用
- hostility decreases: 敌意减少
