1. One of the most effective ways to get your site's pages to rank higher in search engine results is by adding original text to each page. Google crawls and indexes each page individually, and ranks pages higher based on their relevance to certain keywords. ("Optimizing Your Site's Content for SEO," 2018)

> 让你的网站页面在搜索引擎结果中排名更高的最有效方法之一是在每个页面上添加原创文本。谷歌单独爬取和索引每个页面，并根据它们与某些关键词的相关性对页面进行排名。

- index: 索引，编入索引

- effective ways: 有效方法

- site's pages: 网站页面

- rank higher: 排名更高

- search engine results: 搜索引擎结果

- adding original text: 添加原创文本

- Google: 谷歌

- crawls: 爬取

- individually: 单独地

- ranks: 排名

- based on: 基于

- relevance: 相关性

- keywords: 关键词

3. In fact, from Siquijor, no one could reach Manila directly, as the journey required a boat or plane connection on the island of Negros or in Cebu City. Most islanders were peasants and fishermen, too poor to have had this opportunity. For them, not only was Manila far away but it indexed the very notion of distance. (Dumont, 1995)

> 事实上，从锡基霍尔，没有人能直接到达马尼拉，因为旅程需要在内格罗斯岛或宿务市转乘船只或飞机。大多数岛民是农民和渔民，太穷了，没有这个机会。对他们来说，马尼拉不仅遥远，而且它代表了距离的概念本身。

- index: 代表，象征

- in fact: 事实上

- reach: 到达

- directly: 直接地

- journey: 旅程

- required: 需要

- boat or plane connection: 船只或飞机转乘

- island: 岛

- islanders: 岛民

- peasants: 农民

- fishermen: 渔民

- too poor: 太穷

- opportunity: 机会

- far away: 遥远

- notion: 概念

- distance: 距离
