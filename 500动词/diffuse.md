1. The British political theorist Thomas Paine (1737–1809) proclaimed that the revolution had done more to enlighten the world and diffused a spirit of freedom among humankind than any event that had preceded it.  (Fiero, 2011d)

> 英国政治理论家托马斯·潘恩（1737-1809）宣称，这场革命在启迪世界和在人类中传播自由精神方面比任何先于它的事件做得更多。

- diffuse: 传播
- British political theorist: 英国政治理论家
- Thomas Paine: 托马斯·潘恩
- 1737–1809: 1737-1809年
- proclaimed: 宣称
- revolution: 革命
- done more to: 在...方面做得更多
- enlighten: 启迪
- world: 世界
- spirit of freedom: 自由精神
- among humankind: 在人类中
- any event: 任何事件
- preceded it: 先于它

2. Everyone who wants to learn should be provided with access to available resources at any time in their lives, not just in their childhood or adolescent years. Such a system should make it possible for knowledge to be widely diffused and shared, not confined to specialists. (Giddens, 2009)

> 每个想要学习的人都应该在生活中的任何时候都能获得可用资源，而不仅仅是在童年或青少年时期。这样的系统应该使知识能够广泛传播和共享，而不是局限于专家。

- diffuse: 传播
- everyone: 每个人
- wants to learn: 想要学习
- provided with: 提供
- access to: 获得
- available resources: 可用资源
- any time: 任何时候
- their lives: 他们的生活
- not just: 不仅仅
- childhood: 童年
- adolescent years: 青少年时期
- such a system: 这样的系统
- make it possible: 使...可能
- knowledge: 知识
- widely: 广泛地
- shared: 共享
- confined to: 局限于
- specialists: 专家

3. Belief in such values as equality between men and women, the right to speak freely, democratic participation in government and the pursuit of pleasure through consumption are readily diffused throughout the world over the Internet. (Giddens, 2009)

> 对诸如男女平等、自由发言权、民主参与政府和通过消费追求快乐等价值观的信仰通过互联网在世界各地迅速传播。

- diffuse: 传播
- belief in: 对...的信仰
- such values as: 诸如...的价值观
- equality between men and women: 男女平等
- right to speak freely: 自由发言权
- democratic participation: 民主参与
- government: 政府
- pursuit of pleasure: 追求快乐
- through consumption: 通过消费
- readily: 迅速地
- throughout the world: 世界各地
- over the Internet: 通过互联网
