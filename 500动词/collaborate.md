1. Although Jordaens was never formally a student of Rubens, he was a member of his workshop, and he collaborated with the older artist, turning to him for inspiration throughout his career. (Davies et al., 2015)

  

2. Jan Brueghel also collaborated with other artists and was a noted flower painter. (Davies et al., 2015)

  

3. Balanchine was deeply influenced by the musical innovations of his friend and compatriot, Igor Stravinsky, with whom he often collaborated—Stravinsky wrote the scores for at least four of his ballets. (Fiero, 2011)

  

4. Designers also collaborated with architects, who became more involved in interior decoration.
