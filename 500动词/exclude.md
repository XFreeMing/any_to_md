1. For Mill, it is not freedom that is sacrificed but equality. Certain citizens are to be excluded from the franchise, on educational or economic grounds, while others are given more than one vote. (Wolff, 2006)

> 对密尔来说，被牺牲的不是自由而是平等。某些公民将被排除在选举权之外，基于教育或经济理由，而其他人则被给予多于一票的权利。

- exclude: 排除
- Mill: 密尔
- freedom: 自由
- sacrificed: 被牺牲
- equality: 平等
- certain citizens: 某些公民
- franchise: 选举权
- educational grounds: 教育理由
- economic grounds: 经济理由
- while: 而
- given: 被给予
- more than one vote: 多于一票

2. Unlike the Catholics and other Protestants, most Anabaptists believed in the complete separation of church and state. Not only was government to be excluded from the realm of religion, but it was not even supposed to exercise political jurisdiction over true Christians. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 与天主教徒和其他新教徒不同，大多数再洗礼派信徒相信教会和国家的完全分离。不仅政府要被排除在宗教领域之外，而且它甚至不应该对真正的基督徒行使政治管辖权。

- exclude: 排除
- unlike: 与...不同
- Catholics: 天主教徒
- Protestants: 新教徒
- Anabaptists: 再洗礼派信徒
- believed in: 相信
- complete separation: 完全分离
- church and state: 教会和国家
- not only: 不仅
- government: 政府
- realm of religion: 宗教领域
- but: 而且
- even supposed to: 甚至应该
- exercise: 行使
- political jurisdiction: 政治管辖权
- true Christians: 真正的基督徒

3. By the 1850s, the most prevalent use of photography was for recording the world: people, sights, and objects. These pictures were generally viewed as fact, which is ironic since, as we shall see, photographers could manipulate images in various ways, including the selection of motifs and objects to be included in, or excluded from, a photograph. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 到19世纪50年代，摄影最普遍的用途是记录世界：人物、风景和物体。这些图片通常被视为事实，这是具有讽刺意味的，因为正如我们将看到的，摄影师可以以各种方式操纵图像，包括选择要包含在照片中或从照片中排除的主题和物体。

- exclude: 排除
- by the 1850s: 到19世纪50年代
- most prevalent use: 最普遍的用途
- photography: 摄影
- recording: 记录
- world: 世界
- people: 人物
- sights: 风景
- objects: 物体
- pictures: 图片
- generally viewed as: 通常被视为
- fact: 事实
- ironic: 具有讽刺意味的
- since: 因为
- as we shall see: 正如我们将看到的
- photographers: 摄影师
- manipulate: 操纵
- images: 图像
- various ways: 各种方式
- including: 包括
- selection: 选择
- motifs: 主题
- included in: 包含在...中
- excluded from: 从...中排除
- photograph: 照片

4. Cavendish was not a popularizer of science for women but a participant in the crucial scientific debates of her time. Despite her achievement, however, she was excluded from membership in the Royal Society.

> 卡文迪什不是为女性普及科学的人，而是她那个时代关键科学辩论的参与者。然而，尽管她取得了成就，她还是被排除在皇家学会的会员资格之外。

- exclude: 排除
- Cavendish: 卡文迪什
- popularizer: 普及者
- science: 科学
- women: 女性
- participant: 参与者
- crucial: 关键的
- scientific debates: 科学辩论
- her time: 她的时代
- despite: 尽管
- achievement: 成就
- however: 然而
- membership: 会员资格
- Royal Society: 皇家学会
