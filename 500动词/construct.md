1. The individual parts of the temple were constructed in accordance with certain mathematical ratios also found in nature. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 神庙的各个部分是根据在自然界中也发现的某些数学比例建造的。

- constructed: 建造
- individual parts: 各个部分
- temple: 神庙
- in accordance with: 根据
- certain mathematical ratios: 某些数学比例
- also found in nature: 在自然界中也发现的

2. Plato's search for the just state led him to construct an ideal state in which the population was divided into three basic groups. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 柏拉图对正义国家的探索使他构建了一个理想国家，其中人口被分为三个基本群体。

- construct: 构建
- Plato's search: 柏拉图的探索
- just state: 正义国家
- led him to: 使他
- ideal state: 理想国家
- in which: 其中
- population: 人口
- was divided into: 被分为
- three basic groups: 三个基本群体

3. Builders were held responsible for the buildings they constructed. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 建造者对他们建造的建筑物负责。

- constructed: 建造
- builders: 建造者
- were held responsible for: 对...负责
- buildings: 建筑物
- they: 他们

4. In this famous Funeral Oration, Pericles gave voice to the ideal of democracy and the importance of the individual. It was the Greeks who constructed the intellectual foundations of our Western heritage. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 在这篇著名的葬礼演说中，伯里克利表达了民主的理想和个人的重要性。是希腊人构建了我们西方传统的知识基础。

- constructed: 构建
- famous Funeral Oration: 著名的葬礼演说
- Pericles: 伯里克利
- gave voice to: 表达了
- ideal of democracy: 民主的理想
- importance of the individual: 个人的重要性
- Greeks: 希腊人
- intellectual foundations: 知识基础
- Western heritage: 西方传统
