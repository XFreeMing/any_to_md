1. In many ways, homosexuality has become more normalized - more of an accepted part of everyday society, with many countries passing legislation to protect the rights of homosexuals. (Giddens, 2009)

> 在许多方面，同性恋已经变得更加正常化——更多地成为日常社会中被接受的一部分，许多国家通过了立法来保护同性恋者的权利。

- normalize: 正常化
- in many ways: 在许多方面
- homosexuality: 同性恋
- become more: 变得更加
- accepted part: 被接受的部分
- everyday society: 日常社会
- many countries: 许多国家
- passing legislation: 通过立法
- protect: 保护
- rights: 权利
- homosexuals: 同性恋者

2. Since the late 1960s, many homosexuals have acknowledged their homosexuality openly, and, as we saw in the discussion of gay marriage above, in some areas the lives of homosexual men and women have to a large extent been normalized (Seidman, 1997). (Giddens, 2009)

> 自20世纪60年代末以来，许多同性恋者公开承认他们的同性恋，正如我们在上面关于同性婚姻的讨论中看到的，在一些地区，同性恋男女的生活在很大程度上已经正常化（塞德曼，1997）。

- normalize: 正常化
- since: 自...以来
- late 1960s: 20世纪60年代末
- many homosexuals: 许多同性恋者
- acknowledged: 承认
- homosexuality: 同性恋
- openly: 公开地
- discussion: 讨论
- gay marriage: 同性婚姻
- some areas: 一些地区
- lives: 生活
- homosexual men and women: 同性恋男女
- to a large extent: 在很大程度上

3. For example, to encourage breastfeeding, the law could mandate accommodations that normalize the behavior as natural, and not a shameful activity to be hidden away. (Zamir & Teichman, 2014)

> 例如，为了鼓励母乳喂养，法律可以强制要求将这种行为正常化为自然的，而不是需要隐藏的可耻活动。

- normalize: 正常化
- for example: 例如
- encourage: 鼓励
- breastfeeding: 母乳喂养
- law: 法律
- mandate: 强制要求
- accommodations: 便利设施
- behavior: 行为
- natural: 自然的
- shameful activity: 可耻的活动
- hidden away: 隐藏

4. "The human ability to normalize the abnormal is frightening indeed," observes sociologist Rainer C. Baum.8 Mass murderers need not be psychopaths. It is a "disturbing psychological truth," states Robert Jay Lifton, that "ordinary people can commit demonic acts." (Perry, 2010)

> "人类将异常正常化的能力确实令人恐惧，"社会学家雷纳·C·鲍姆观察到。大规模杀人犯不一定是精神病患者。罗伯特·杰伊·利夫顿指出，这是一个"令人不安的心理真相"，即"普通人可以犯下恶魔般的行为"。

- human ability: 人类能力
- abnormal: 异常的
- frightening indeed: 确实令人恐惧
- observes: 观察到
- sociologist: 社会学家
- Rainer C. Baum: 雷纳·C·鲍姆
