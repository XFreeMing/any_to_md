2. In The Nature of Prejudice, Allport catalogued 15 possible effects of victimization. Allport believed these reactions were reducible to two basic types—those that involve blaming oneself (withdrawal, self-hate, aggression against one's own group) and those that involve blaming external causes (fighting back, suspiciousness, increased group pride). (Myers, 2012)

> 在《偏见的本质》中，奥尔波特编目了15种可能的受害效应。奥尔波特认为这些反应可以归结为两种基本类型——那些涉及责备自己的（退缩、自我憎恨、对自己群体的攻击）和那些涉及责备外部原因的（反击、怀疑、增加的群体自豪感）。

- catalogue: 编目

- The Nature of Prejudice: 偏见的本质

- Allport: 奥尔波特

- possible effects: 可能的效应

- victimization: 受害

- believed: 认为

- reactions: 反应

- reducible to: 可归结为

- two basic types: 两种基本类型

- involve: 涉及

- blaming oneself: 责备自己

- withdrawal: 退缩

- self-hate: 自我憎恨

- aggression: 攻击

- one's own group: 自己的群体

- external causes: 外部原因

- fighting back: 反击

- suspiciousness: 怀疑

- increased group pride: 增加的群体自豪感

  

3. Living in Periclean Athens, whose lifeblood was politics, Thucydides (Greek historian) regarded the motives of statesmen and the acts of government as the essence of history. He did not just catalogue facts but sought those general concepts and principles that the facts illustrated.

> 生活在政治是其生命线的伯里克利时代的雅典，修昔底德（希腊历史学家）将政治家的动机和政府的行为视为历史的本质。他不仅仅是编目事实，而是寻求事实所说明的那些一般概念和原则。

- catalogue: 编目

- living in: 生活在

- Periclean Athens: 伯里克利时代的雅典

- lifeblood: 生命线

- statesmen: 政治家

- essence: 本质

- history: 历史

- facts: 事实

- sought: 寻求

- general concepts: 一般概念

- principles: 原则

- illustrated: 说明
