1. More recently, Connell has begun to examine the effects of globalization on the gender order. She argues that gender itself has become globalized. (Giddens, 2009)

> 最近，康奈尔开始研究全球化对性别秩序的影响。她认为性别本身已经全球化了。

- globalize: 全球化
- more recently: 最近
- Connell: 康奈尔
- begun to examine: 开始研究
- effects: 影响
- globalization: 全球化
- gender order: 性别秩序
- argues: 认为
- gender itself: 性别本身
- become: 成为

2. While some Japanese companies have publicly said they will hire more foreigners in a bid to globalize their work forces, they remain a minority. (Williamson, 2013)

> 虽然一些日本公司公开表示他们将雇用更多外国人以试图使其劳动力全球化，但它们仍然是少数。

- globalize: 全球化
- while: 虽然
- some Japanese companies: 一些日本公司
- publicly said: 公开表示
- hire: 雇用
- more foreigners: 更多外国人
- bid: 试图
- work forces: 劳动力
- remain: 仍然是
- minority: 少数

3. One way to lower costs will be to forge alliances with foreign companies or to expand internationally through appropriate takeovers – in short, to "globalize". (McConnell, Brue, & Flynn, 2014)

> 降低成本的一种方法将是与外国公司建立联盟或通过适当的收购进行国际扩张——简而言之，就是"全球化"。

- globalize: 全球化
- one way: 一种方法
- lower costs: 降低成本
- forge alliances: 建立联盟
- foreign companies: 外国公司
- expand internationally: 国际扩张
- through: 通过
- appropriate takeovers: 适当的收购
- in short: 简而言之

4. The media industry has become globalized over the past three decades. (Giddens, 2009)

> 媒体行业在过去三十年中已经全球化了。

- globalize: 全球化
- media industry: 媒体行业
- become: 成为
- over the past three decades: 在过去三十年中
