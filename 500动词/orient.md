1. A new map program has been created to both orient students to the locations mentioned in each chapter and to better tell the story of the chapter narrative. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 已经创建了一个新的地图程序，既可以帮助学生了解每章中提到的位置，也可以更好地讲述章节叙述的故事。

- orient: 使...了解
- new map program: 新的地图程序
- created: 创建
- both: 既
- students: 学生
- locations: 位置
- mentioned: 提到的
- each chapter: 每章
- better: 更好地
- tell: 讲述
- story: 故事
- chapter narrative: 章节叙述

2. Technological revolutions in the 20th century have led to the invention of a newly created media environment, where we learn to orient ourselves in cyberspace. (Haviland, Prins, McBride, & Walrath, 2013)

> 20世纪的技术革命导致了新创建的媒体环境的发明，我们在其中学习在网络空间中定位自己。

- orient: 定位
- technological revolutions: 技术革命
- 20th century: 20世纪
- led to: 导致
- invention: 发明
- newly created: 新创建的
- media environment: 媒体环境
- learn to: 学习
- ourselves: 自己
- cyberspace: 网络空间

3. The rerun elected a reform candidate, Victor Yuschenko, who pledged to orient his country toward Western Europe and away from Moscow. (Perry, 2010)

> 重新选举选出了一位改革候选人维克多·尤先科，他承诺将他的国家导向西欧，远离莫斯科。

- orient: 导向
- rerun: 重新选举
- elected: 选出
- reform candidate: 改革候选人
- Victor Yuschenko: 维克多·尤先科
- pledged: 承诺
- country: 国家
- toward: 朝向
- Western Europe: 西欧
- away from: 远离
- Moscow: 莫斯科

4. Its four sides are almost precisely oriented to the four points of the compass. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 它的四个边几乎精确地朝向指南针的四个方向。

- orient: 朝向
- four sides: 四个边
- almost precisely: 几乎精确地
- four points: 四个方向
- compass: 指南针
