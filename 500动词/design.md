1. Detailed map captions are designed to enrich students' awareness of the importance of geography to history, and numerous spot maps enable students to see at a glance the region or subject being discussed in the text. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 详细的地图说明旨在丰富学生对地理对历史重要性的认识，许多位置图使学生能够一目了然地看到文本中正在讨论的地区或主题。

- designed: 设计，旨在
- detailed map captions: 详细的地图说明
- enrich: 丰富
- students' awareness: 学生的认识
- importance of geography: 地理的重要性
- to history: 对历史
- numerous spot maps: 许多位置图
- enable students: 使学生能够
- see at a glance: 一目了然地看到
- region: 地区
- subject: 主题
- being discussed: 正在讨论的
- in the text: 在文本中

2. Europeans also developed windmills to capture the power of the wind. Historians are unsure if windmills were imported into Europe (they were invented in Persia) or designed independently by Europeans. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 欧洲人也开发了风车来捕获风力。历史学家不确定风车是进口到欧洲的（它们在波斯发明）还是由欧洲人独立设计的。

- designed: 设计
- Europeans: 欧洲人
- developed: 开发
- windmills: 风车
- capture: 捕获
- power of the wind: 风力
- historians: 历史学家
- unsure: 不确定
- imported into Europe: 进口到欧洲
- invented in Persia: 在波斯发明
- independently: 独立地
- by Europeans: 由欧洲人

3. The enactment of public health and housing measures, designed to curb the worst ills of urban living, was yet another indication of how state power could be used to benefit the people. (Spielvogel, 2010)

> 公共卫生和住房措施的实施，旨在遏制城市生活最严重的弊病，是国家权力如何被用来造福人民的又一个迹象。

- designed: 设计，旨在
- enactment: 实施
- public health: 公共卫生
- housing measures: 住房措施
- curb: 遏制
- worst ills: 最严重的弊病
- urban living: 城市生活
- yet another indication: 又一个迹象
- state power: 国家权力
- could be used: 可以被用来
- benefit the people: 造福人民

> 英国数学家艾伦·图灵设计了一台原始计算机，以协助英国情报部门破解德国密码机的秘密代码。

- designed: 设计
- British mathematician: 英国数学家
- Alan Turing: 艾伦·图灵
- primitive computer: 原始计算机
- assist: 协助
- British intelligence: 英国情报部门
- breaking: 破解
- secret codes: 秘密代码
- German ciphering machines: 德国密码机
