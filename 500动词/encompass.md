1. German Expressionism surfaced as a cohesive movement toward 1905, and although it encompassed a range of issues and styles, it can be characterized as tortured, anguished, brutally primitive, or passionately spiritual, reflecting elemental cosmic forces. (Davies et al., 2015)

> 德国表现主义在1905年左右作为一个有凝聚力的运动出现，虽然它涵盖了一系列问题和风格，但可以描述为痛苦的、痛苦的、残酷原始的或充满激情的灵性，反映了基本的宇宙力量。

- encompass: 涵盖，包含

- surfaced: 出现

- cohesive movement: 有凝聚力的运动

- range of issues: 一系列问题

- styles: 风格

- characterized: 描述为

- tortured: 痛苦的

- anguished: 痛苦的

- brutally primitive: 残酷原始的

- passionately spiritual: 充满激情的灵性

- reflecting: 反映

- elemental cosmic forces: 基本的宇宙力量

  

2. The study of caring activities is a third area where feminists have made important contributions. This is a broad realm which encompasses a variety of processes, from attending to a family member who is ill to looking after an elderly relative over a long period of time. (Giddens, 2009)

> 关怀活动的研究是女权主义者做出重要贡献的第三个领域。这是一个广泛的领域，涵盖了各种过程，从照顾生病的家庭成员到长期照顾年长的亲戚。

- encompass: 涵盖，包括

- caring activities: 关怀活动

- feminists: 女权主义者

- contributions: 贡献

- broad realm: 广泛的领域

- variety of processes: 各种过程

- attending to: 照顾

- elderly relative: 年长的亲戚

- long period of time: 长期

  

3. Another major concern of art historians is, of course, subject matter, encompassing the story, or narrative; the scene presented; the action's time and place; the persons involved; and the environment and its details. (Kleiner, 2015)

> 艺术史学家的另一个主要关注点当然是主题，包括故事或叙述；呈现的场景；行动的时间和地点；涉及的人物；以及环境及其细节。

- encompass: 包括，涵盖

- major concern: 主要关注点

- art historians: 艺术史学家

- subject matter: 主题

- narrative: 叙述

- scene: 场景

- presented: 呈现的

- action: 行动

- involved: 涉及的

- environment: 环境

- details: 细节

  

4. Although the region has no formal name or administrative structure, by 1995 it had already encompassed a population of 50 million people. (Giddens, 2009)

> 尽管该地区没有正式名称或行政结构，但到1995年，它已经包含了5000万人口。

- encompass: 包含，涵盖

- region: 地区

- formal name: 正式名称

- administrative structure: 行政结构

- population: 人口
